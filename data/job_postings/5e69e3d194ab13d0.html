Principal Data Scientist - Tempe, AZ 85281

Symantec Corporation (NASDAQ: SYMC), a global leader in cybersecurity, operates one of the world’s largest cyber intelligence networks allowing organizations, governments, and people to secure their most important data wherever it lives. Enterprises across the world rely on Symantec for integrated cyber defense against sophisticated attacks across endpoints, infrastructure, and cloud. More than 50 million people and families rely on Symantec’s Norton and LifeLock Digital Safety Platform to help protect their personal information, devices, home networks, and identities at home and across their devices.

We are looking for a hands-on, results-oriented, intellectually curious, self-learning Principal Data Scientist to join our Data Science team. Our mission is to discover actionable insights (inference, prediction, relationships, etc.) regarding our customers and use that knowledge to create data products (predictive models, classifiers, clusters, recommenders, etc.) that will improve our products, customer experience, and business performance.

Although we expect you to have experience using a variety of modeling and algorithmic methods, we also expect you to apply strong business acumen to the models you build. You possess excellent communication skills and can engage with senior leaders and work cross-functionally with product managers, and data and application engineers to integrate your results into our operations. We work with a variety of stakeholders such as Marketing, Customer Service, Sales, and Product Engineering.

Responsibilities

  • Learn and stay current with the latest techniques in statistical and machine learning and apply them to our business challenges
  • Learn our data model and the underlying physics that it represents
  • Identify and analyze customer behaviors for use in tactical and strategic decision-making and integration into our processes, operations, and products
  • Develop predictive models, classifiers, and other data products that will be used for personalization and automation in a variety of contexts
  • Work with Engineering and Product teams to ensure the appropriate metrics and dimensions are incorporated into our data sets and to integrate our data products into our infrastructure

Qualifications

  • BS, Masters, or PhD degree in a quantitative discipline (Economics, Finance, Statistics, Chemistry, Physics, Biology, Mathematics, Engineering, Computer Science, or MBA with emphasis in analytics)
  • 5+ years' work experience in a data science role
  • Proficient at creating and analyzing large multi-dimensional data sets using open-source, parallel-processing frameworks, such as Apache Spark
  • Proficient with Python, SQL, analytical notebooks such as Jupyter and Data Bricks
  • Expert on Machine Learning supervised and unsupervised methods: regression and classification techniques (Random Forest, SVM, Gradient Boosting algorithms, Apriori, K-Means clustering), stochastic methods, Natural Language Processing, etc
  • Ability to build, test, deploy and iterate statistical models and report on model performance
  • Experience with Microsoft Azure HDInsight technology stack is a plus
  • Translating data into an informed story via visualization tools such as Qlikview or Tableau
  • Excellent communication skills with strong ability to synthesize key insights into actionable solutions
