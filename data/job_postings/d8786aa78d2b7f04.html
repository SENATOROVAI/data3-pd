Business Reporting and Data Analyst - Westlake Village, CA

COMPANY BACKGROUND/CULTURE

AmeriHome was founded in November of 2013 by a small group of experienced mortgage professionals with deep financial, correspondent, secondary and risk management experience. AmeriHome has maintained its client centric core principles throughout its rapid ascent to a top 5 correspondent investor and continues to look into ways to deepen its partnership with its clients.


KEY RESPONSIBILITIES

Under the direction of the VP, Business Analytics Manager, the Business Reporting and Data Analyst will work closely with the staff, technical team members, project managers, business users, and IT staff to deliver from minor enhancements to complex projects. The individual will be expected to research and analyze information, anticipate and recognize problems and opportunities, creatively develop possible solutions, evaluate alternatives and, using sound judgment, determine best course of action. The ideal candidate will be a driven individual who is logical, analytical and detailed oriented, has some mortgage experience, and has a good understanding of database administration. This position will be part of the CL Business Analytics team, which develops reporting and routines to ensure consistent communication between Correspondent Lending and related corporate counterparties. The Business Reporting and Data Analyst will:

  • Compile analytical and statistical reports as needed
  • Formulate, define and recommend scope and format of reports
  • Oversee provision of ad hoc management reports
  • Review information for accuracy and reconciles data on a regular basis
  • Provide internal and external data needs
  • Define, review and implement best practices for data entry, software security, reporting and information management within database
  • Serve as a subject matter expert on Salesforce platform; stay up-to-date on new SFDC products
  • Map and load data into development environments
  • Elicit business and functional requirements and document them in JIRA / Confluence / Atlassian
  • Develops and creates management reporting, including monthly business review materials and presentations
  • Perform at a high departmental service levels, production, and quality standards
  • Applies applicable theories and concepts to interpret data, provide analysis and observations
  • Demonstrate an understanding of business drivers and how they influence the Salesforce design
  • Implement new projects in Salesforce, interfacing with other systems in the organization

PROFESSIONAL EXPERIENCE/QUALIFICATIONS

  • Proven analytical and problem solving skills
  • Thorough understanding of frameworks, analytics, programming languages, design, databases and operating systems
  • Ability to work independently and as a team player
  • Organized, responsible, and ability to use good judgment
  • Ability to thrive in a fast paced, multi-tasking environment, and to manage multiple deadlines
  • Effective at managing multiple projects/tasks of varying complexities, meeting deadlines and working well under pressure
  • Demonstrated verbal and written communication skills
  • Proficiency in Microsoft Office products (Excel, Word, PowerPoint, Visio)
  • Prior experience in relational databases, Microsoft SQL Server, and Excel VBA is a plus
  • Certification as Salesforce Developer preferred
  • A firm understanding of mortgage banking, correspondent lending, and mortgage finance is a plus
