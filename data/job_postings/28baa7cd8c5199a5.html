Data Scientist Senior - Canastota, NY

Data Scientist Senior

Canastota, NY

  • Position Title : Data Scientist Senior
  • Employment Type: Full-Time

The Job:

Use your machine learning and coding know-how on a daily basis to design and develop techniques for improved and autonomous multi-platform SIGINT, ISR, UAS, and cyber operation systems.

You will excel in this role if you have:

  • A love of machine learning and a knack for coding and scripting
  • A problem-solving nature
  • The ability to change directions and work on the fly

Problem types you will work on:

  • Design original deep learning models for automated analysis of RF signals
  • Identify data needs, helping procure or generate data, curating and preparing data for training models
  • Build a high-throughput data pipeline for training in a distributed environment
  • Design and implement sophisticated hyper-parameter tuning strategies to optimize existing deep learning models
  • Build a test suite for thoroughly characterizing the performance of machine learning models in a variety of RF environments
  • Integrate deep learning models with conventional (expertise-driven) analysis tool-chains and processing pipelines
  • Deploy models to portable platforms (e.g., Raspberry Pi)

What we expect from you, i.e., your qualifications:

  • Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Information Science, Mathematics, Physics or related discipline and 10+ years of experience in that field. Master’s degree with 6 - 9 years of experience
  • Proficiency in Python
  • Strong experience with neural networks/deep learning
  • Experience with other machine learning and data modeling techniques such as decision trees, random forests, K-means, SVM, and incremental response
  • Strong foundation in statistical tests and procedures
  • Experience with survey sampling methodologies and data collection techniques
  • Ability to lead small-sized teams
  • Applicants selected will be subject to a government security investigation and must meet eligibility requirements for access to classified information
U.S. citizenship is required.

Nice to haves:

  • Experience with neural networks for Natural Language Processing (NPL) applications, time-series analysis and/or sequence analysis
  • Experience with Tensorflow and/or Keras
  • Experience with SciPy, NumPY and Pandas python packages
  • Experience with R and the tidyverse ecosystem
  • Experience with developing in a Linux environment
  • Knowledge of radio communication technologies, i.e., coursework, amateur radio, etc
  • Knowledge of or past experience working within an agile environment
  • Experience with writing government proposals
  • Active security clearance
