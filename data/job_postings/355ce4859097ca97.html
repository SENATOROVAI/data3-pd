Data Scientist - Denton, TX 76201

The Data, Analytics, and Institutional Research (DAIR) Department at the University of North Texas is seeking to hire a creative and analytically focused Data Scientist to join our team of nationally recognized practitioners. Responsibilities of the position include, but are not limited to: Purpose/Description Partners with a variety of internal and external stakeholders to perform advanced data design and analysis using a broad array of data science skills and data structures to create new knowledge useful to the university. This position proactively identifies, develops, and implements analytical and institutional data science applications to find creative solutions to medium to long term issues facing the university and our students. Leverages programming, data architecture activities, and analytic tools to respond to complex challenges to drive lift and ROI via actionable insights. Representative Tasks
  • Works with C-Suite leaders, senior administrators, Deans, staff, external constituents, and internal DAIR colleagues to formulate and scope questions to assist leaders in higher education transformation
  • Develops predictive models to help leaders solve critical higher education problems
  • Influences a general audience to understand the quality, completeness, and appropriate use of data science approaches and provides advice about choice of statistical and machine learning approaches
  • Develops tools and libraries to create efficiencies for future work
  • Working in partnership across the DAIR office, identifies new sources of data within the UNT data landscape that will improve information about student and administrative outcomes
  • Stay at the forefront of emerging analytics and data science issues in higher education by actively representing the University at conferences or meetings
