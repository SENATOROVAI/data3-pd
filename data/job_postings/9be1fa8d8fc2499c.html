Description
The Texas Department of Insurance (TDI) is a state agency that regulates the insurance industry, administers the Texas workers’ compensation system, and works to educate and protect consumers. Among other responsibilities, our agency is charged to protect and ensure the fair treatment of consumers, as well as ensure fair competition in the insurance industry to foster a competitive market.

Data Analyst Intern (2 positions):
The internship would involve conducting detailed statistical analysis of the insurance market, particularly related to the behavior of insurance companies and insurance agents. The goal of this effort would be to determine which factors are the greatest predictors of fraud or other negative behavior in the marketplace. This would involve conducting analysis in the field of predictive analytics, to be determined by TDI staff and the interns, but potentially involving Monte Carlo Simulation, Sensitivity Analysis, Linear Regression or other statistical techniques deemed feasible and appropriate. Datasets include application and registration data as well as consumer complaints and potentially social media activity. Staff with related and relevant job duties will provide direct and regular supervision and feedback within the co-working space.

We are looking for candidates with:
  • Knowledge of quantitative research methods, advanced statistical principles
  • Statistics knowledge (p-values, linear model assumptions, logistical regression)
  • Experience with software applications, such as SQL, Oracle Database, R, SAS, Python, Microsoft Office
  • Experience with Machine learning (regression, classification, tree-based methods)
  • Skill in identifying and formatting applicable datasets
  • Skill in performing advanced data analysis
  • Skill in problem solving, decision making, planning
  • Ability to organize and prioritize multiple competing projects
  • Effective written and oral communication
  • Successful candidates will be enthusiastic, self-motivated, flexible, and have good written and verbal communication skills
