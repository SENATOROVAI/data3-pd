Data Scientist - Generalist

Summary:

Our data science team is expanding! The successful candidate will work with the data science and engineering teams on challenging projects in energy, logistics, healthcare, or finance.


Responsibilities and Duties:

  • Ingest, organize, and analyze data from various sources (e.g. CSV, relational database)
  • Use data to inform and label customer outcomes and processes
  • Scope unstructured problems or messy data for tractable insights
  • Identify candidate statistical or analytic solutions and test their efficacy
  • Communicate technical work and findings both verbally and through visualization
  • Work in a collaborative environment to brainstorm, design, implement and deliver solutions to challenging problems

Required Qualifications:

  • 3+ years of relevant work experience
  • Excellent understanding of statistical or machine learning techniques, such as clustering, linear/logistic regression, time series forecasting, tree-based methods, sampling methods, support vector machines
  • Demonstrated ability using scientific computing libraries, such as NumPy, Pandas, SciPy, Scikit-learn, and matplotlib
  • Programming proficiency in Python or R

Preferred Experience:

  • PhD or Master’s
  • Strong statistical knowledge
  • Experience with experimental design and hypothesis testing
  • Research experience with high impact publications
  • Development of data science solutions for industrial projects
  • Domain expertise in energy, logistics, healthcare, or finance
  • Experience working with cross-functional teams
