Cyber Intelligence Analyst - Charlottesville, VA 22911

Job Description

CACI is looking for a Cyber Intelligence Analyst to work on a multi-functional team. In this position, you will be in support of the Facilities and Infrastructure Characterization and Analysis (FICA) Branch at the National Ground Intelligence Center (NGIC). Candidate will provide Cyber Analysis support to a multi-intelligence fused team consisting of other all-source analysts, GEOINT and GIS specialists, SIGINT, and Culture Language analysts, with Technology and Collection Management support.

Required Qualifications:

Position requires a BA, 5 years of experience, and a TS/SCI security clearance.
  • Conducts research, analysis, and correlation across a wide variety of all source cyber threat data sets
  • Provides comprehensive analysis across the full spectrum of the Open Systems Interconnect (OSI) layers 1 - 7 against targeted facilities and infrastructure
  • Researches, identifies, and verifies cyber threat adversary tools, tactics, and processes
  • Performs detailed analysis of cyber targets to identify vulnerabilities and develop recommendations for operational opportunity to exploit those vulnerabilities
  • Develop methodologies, tradecraft, and procedures for conducting facility centric cyber analysis against targeted infrastructure
  • Maintains currency of Advanced Persistent Threat (APT) activities
  • Performs CND trend analysis and threat intelligence reporting
  • Contributes to the completion of milestones associated with specific projects
  • Provides solutions to a variety of complex technical problems

Desired Qualifications:

  • Experience with structured analytic tools such as Palantir
  • Familiarity with a variety of the Social Network Analysis (SNA) concepts, practices and procedures
  • Familiarity with critical infrastructure directives and supply chain risk management
  • Experience with information security and assurance principles and standards (NIST, ISO, etc.) and associated supporting technologies
  • Ability to assess and weigh current and evolving security threats in an operational environment
  • Self-motivated problem solver, ability to learn quickly
