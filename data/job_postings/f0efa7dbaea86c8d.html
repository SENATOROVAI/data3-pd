VSAN Senior Member of Technical Staff - Storage and lifecycle platform - Palo Alto, CA 94304

HCI (Hyper-Converged Infrastructure) software development team is now looking for a seasoned engineer with excellent leadership, analytical skills and domain knowledge to join and be part of developing the leading edge HCI products.

Responsibilities:

  • Understanding the key requirements for the data center virtualization orchestration, architecting a solution and delivering a working prototype and the final product
  • Lead & contribute to architecture, design, and implementation of a distributed platform for container-packaged applications
  • Review and test existing technologies and software components and make recommendations for use and integration
  • Build rapid prototypes and work on fast paced projects
  • Provide technical specifications, talks, presentations and white papers for new designs and architectures
  • Collaborate effectively with other staff to solve complex problems
  • Assist with debugging and verification of defects in your own and others' work
  • Provide mentorship and lead strong development practices inside the team
  • Engage, champion and contribute to Open Source Software communities

Competencies:
  • BS/MS in CS from reputable university or equivalent experience
  • Deep technical depth and hands-on expertise in data center technologies including virtualized storage, computing and networking. network virtualization and orchestration; strong knowledge of Storage Area Network, L2/L3 protocols, VxLAN
  • Hands-on experience with VMware products is highly desired
  • Familiarity with virtualization architectures and protocols; solid understanding of API interfaces
  • Experience with clustering and with building distributed, scalable software systems is highly desirable
  • Exposure to multiple programming languages is essential, as the ability to pick up and use the language that suits a body of work is expected
  • Outstanding coding skills in one or more of those programming languages
  • Must be capable of researching alternatives and making architectural/design/business decisions independently
