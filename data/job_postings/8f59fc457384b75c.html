Data Entry Clerk, Claims - French Camp, CA 95231

Under direct supervision, responsible for receiving incoming/outgoing mail, faxes, scanned images, claims documents, and other documents, and ensuring that items are prepared for processing.

Essential Functions

  • Prepares outgoing correspondence using standard templates to providers, including but not limited to refund requests, provider dispute acknowledgement and determination letters, and documentation to the State of CA
  • Maintains (enter and change status) inventory tracking grid so that claims and batches are logged in and out accurately
  • Performs various data entry, including entering production statistics and certain claim types into tracking grids
  • Reviews routine reports, including unknown member report; enters missing data, as appropriate
  • Files and prepares documents for storage
