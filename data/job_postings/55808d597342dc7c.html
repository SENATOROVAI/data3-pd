About the job

The mission of the Data Science and Engineering (DS&E) team is to drive product excellence and growth with data insights. Our vision is to build a core team with unique expertise in human evaluation, live traffic and user behavior analysis, market insights and strategic analysis, growth best practices, and high-scale evaluation infrastructure and tools.

As the Director of Program Management, you will own and set the strategy and implementation for all the programs across the DS&E organization. You will lead the streamlined product launch processes for Google Search and Assistant and will have a cross-functional and visible leadership role, working horizontally with all teams across the organization. You will lead the Search Human Rating program, execute the strategy and engineering development processes that help build data analytics platforms in the DS&E organization, and enhance the experiment and analysis tools we build to measure Google's product success and safeguard our principles.

Google is and always will be an engineering company. We hire people with a broad set of technical skills who are ready to take on some of technology's greatest challenges and make an impact on millions, if not billions, of users. At Google, engineers not only revolutionize search, they routinely work on massive scalability and storage solutions, large-scale applications and entirely new platforms for developers around the world. From Google Ads to Chrome, Android to YouTube, Social to Local, Google engineers are changing the world one technological achievement after another.

Responsibilities

  • Manage a diverse team, navigate organizational and technical problems, and drive projects and organization strategies. Build consensus and influence decision-making within senior-level audiences
  • Lead the Search and Assistant product launch processes across the organization and create operational processes and practices that drive efficiencies
  • Define and lead strategy and roadmap for Search Human Rating Program, balancing priorities against business needs while managing costs
  • Plan, facilitate, and manage services purchased from third-party suppliers and vendors worldwide. Partner with teams to negotiate the terms of new agreements. Manage vendors to ensure they adhere to SLAs around delivery targets, billing, and technical requirements of the program
  • Work with teams to develop guidelines to produce high quality ratings data consistent with Google policy. Work with data scientists to find quality signals in data, and use those to inform program changes
