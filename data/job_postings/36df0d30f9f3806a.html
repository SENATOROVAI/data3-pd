Confluent is growing at an incredible rate with a huge amount of that growth in our sales organization. We are looking for a Deal Desk Analyst to join our team in Mountain View. Reporting to the Director of Sales Operations, you will have an opportunity to work cross-functionally throughout our organization on a variety of projects. For this role, an analytical mindset and attention to detail are imperative. You will be working directly with our sales team on contracts and pricing.

Responsibilities

  • Manage the day-to-day quote and pricing approvals
  • Partner with Sales to ensure opportunities are properly created and tracked in Salesforce including stage, probability, booking amount, and various other required fields
  • Assist Sales with creating Order Forms
  • Provide proactive support and guidance to the sales team in order to help drive new deals as well as up-sell opportunities
  • Read, interpret, and escalate contractual language to ensure transactions comply with revenue recognition and business operational policies
  • Maintain an agreed upon SLA on deal turnaround times
  • Assist in responding to RFPs, RFQs, RFIs
  • Help drive projects and initiatives focused on improving productivity and simplifying processes throughout the sales operations and sales organization

What we're looking for

  • Experience with both Channel and Direct Sales model is highly desirable
  • Experience with Salesforce CPQ
  • Specific experience with deal structure and quoting tools
  • Must be a self-starter, detail oriented, and analytical with strong organizational skills
  • Extremely strong interpersonal, verbal, and written communication skills
