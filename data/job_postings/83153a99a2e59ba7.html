Summary of Job Purpose:

To be part of an analytics team and work directly with the client on various analytical projects.

To add value to deliverables by bringing in best practice, solving business problems, providing alternate solutions

The broad overview of the role involves- :

  • Being a part of the team working on marketing analytics with global clients

  • Ensure on-time & error free delivery, proactively escalate issues when required
