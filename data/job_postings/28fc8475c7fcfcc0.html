Intern - Data Engineering - Brea, CA

Job description

Job Summary:
The Data Engineering Intern will support Data Engineers in the decision-making process within the organization by extracting relevant data sets and assisting in the corresponding analysis. Under limited supervision they must analyze data and turn it into actionable information. The Data Engineering Intern must be able to communicate results in a coherent manner

Duties and Responsibilities:
 Write SQL queries to produce relevant data sets to be used in statistical analysis and data mining models
 Prepare reports in a presentable fashion that combines data from various sources
 Assist in applying data mining techniques to analyze product performance
 Produce and evaluate performance metrics utilized by the company

Job requirements

Preferred Qualifications and/or Education:
 Pursuing a Bachelor's Degree in Computer Science, Engineering, Mathematics or Statistics
 Ability to apply fundamentals in Probability and Statistics to real data
 Track record of strong mathematical maturity
 Experience in Relational Database Systems and SQL queries
 Experience developing numerical algorithms for solving complex mathematical problems
 Experience using scripting languages such as: Python, Matlab, Ruby, R or SAS
