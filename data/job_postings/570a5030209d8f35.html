Data Science Intern, Burbank - Spring 2020 - Burbank, CA

Walt Disney Television is seeking a Data Science Intern to join the Advanced Analytics team. This high impact internship will allow you to leverage analytical, business and technical BI skills to develop a rich understanding of how consumers interact with our brands, and deliver consumable insights to increase consumer engagement. The ideal intern candidate will have experience in mathematics & statistical techniques used for analyzing data, plus an understanding of consumer behaviors related to marketing, digital experiences, and relationship management.




Responsibilities :
Supporting the Advanced Analytics team within Walt Disney Television, you will assist in building out dashboards that automate existing reports, creating visualizations that allow business users to dive deeper into data, perform analysis to improve marketing campaign performance, and much more!

Basic Qualifications :
  • Experience with Python and/or R
  • Demonstrated experience using SQL to manage large multi-dimensional data sets
  • Knowledge of data visualization tools such as Tableau, Power BI, or Looker
  • Strong oral and written communication skills
  • Experience collecting & analyzing data and reporting on the results
  • Strong analytical and problem solving skills
  • Proficiency in Microsoft Excel including advanced formulas
  • Strong sense of responsibility, ownership, and pride in delivering quality results

Preferred Qualifications:
  • Experience building machine learning models
  • Previous experience analyzing app behavior in News and/or Entertainment
