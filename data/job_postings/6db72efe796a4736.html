Reliability Technician - Milpitas, CA 95035

This is a contract job which may lead to a regular full-time position depending on business needs.

Join Nanosys in the development of next generation quantum dot technology for displays and lighting. Our partners and customers use our industry leading quantum dots to make amazing TVs and other displays. Our next generation products will be even better.

In this role, you will be actively involved in supporting our state of the art optical and reliability laboratories. Responsibilities in this role include but are not limited to: measuring optical and reliability data on Nanosys quantum dot materials; handling and evaluating optical films; placing materials on test; taking measurements at specified timepoints; performing routine analysis and communicating effectively with a multidisciplinary team.

Summary of essential job functions:

  • Measure optical data using Labview and SpectraSuite
  • Perform routine data analysis
  • Perform routine maintenance and monitoring of test and measurement platforms
  • Write work procedures in Word and Excel
  • Communicate verbally and via email with supervisor, test owners, and team members
  • Quickly understand, respond, and adapt to new requests, both verbal and written
  • Quickly learn and adapt new procedures
  • Support other team functions in the Quality Control group as needed; which may include optical characterization involving instruments such as UV Vis spectrophotometer and fluorometer for various testing purposes
  • Ability to work weekend and after-hours work as needed

Required Skills:

  • Computer proficiency and experiencing handling spreadsheets

- Work effectively with Windows and Office programs - Excel, Word, Powerpoint.

  • Understand and operate sensitive measurement equipment
  • Superb ability for accuracy and precision in data collection and measurements
  • Must be able to quickly adapt to new and different methods for the operation of software and equipment
  • Must have exceptionally good organizational skills and pay close attention to detail
  • Must be able to work independently and efficiently

Education & Experience:

  • AS or BS degree in a scientific or engineering discipline; or 5+ years experience in test measurement roles
  • Experience with Labview or Visual basic programming desired
  • Optics, test and measurement, and/or reliability experience a plus
