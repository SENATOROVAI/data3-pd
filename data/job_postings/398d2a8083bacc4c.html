The EHS Specialist III prepares compliance and technical reports to meet Environmental, Health and Safety (EHS) regulatory requirements. Acts as primary site interface with regulatory agencies. Prepares and submits permit applications. Performs program reviews, investigates incidents and provides corrective action to prevent reoccurrence of incidents. Evaluates workplace hazards. Provides input to new business opportunities and facilities projects to ensure compliance. Conducts education and training to support regulatory and business requirements. Evaluates impact of new regulations on company operations. Reviews vendor, contractor and supplier activities for EHS compliance for site processes and program goals in our Irving, TX location.
