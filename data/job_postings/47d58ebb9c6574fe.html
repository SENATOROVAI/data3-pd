Job Family Summary

The Claim Reserve department is responsible for assuring that Allstate’s reserves for Property-Liability losses and claims expense are adequate, accurately recorded, and properly disclosed.

Job Description

The Sr. Actuarial Consultant - Reserve Analytics is responsible for developing information and providing actuarial analysis necessary for the estimation of loss and expense reserves for multiple lines of business. In addition, the position is responsible for developing recommendations for moderately complex reserve position action plans and implementing complex reserve position action plans. The Senior Consultant works closely with the business units to communicate trends, ultimate loss projections, and reserve position action plans. Pursuing actuarial designation and has completed at least two actuarial exams, 3 or more preferred.

Key Responsibilities
  • Performs moderate to highly complex actuarial/statistical loss reserving techniques and analysis for modeling future loss and expense payments on incurred claims to determine the ultimate loss and expense estimates for multiple lines of business
  • Provides observations on moderately complex trend analysis (e.g. CWA, CWP, salvage and subrogation ratios) and applies those observations in determining estimates of ultimate losses
  • Performs statistical curve fitting to determine tail factors for loss development methods
  • Reviews and makes observations of appropriateness of moderately complex actuarial loss reserving techniques for specific lines of business based on trends identified in the underlying data
  • Performs basic to moderately complex statistical analysis related to the measurement of the variability of the reserve estimate (e.g. Mack Method and Bootstrapping)
  • Serves as a subject matter expert for their respective discipline from an internal perspective
  • Actively participates in actuarial development within the department
  • Develops more complex information and analysis necessary to support completion of department objectives and projects
  • Estimates reserve position and develops recommendations for moderately complex reserve position action plans
  • Gathers feedback from the business units on reserve position findings
  • Contributes to the communication of reserve action plans to the business unit and other key stakeholders
  • Implements approved complex reserve position action plans
  • Provides consultation on reserving issues
  • Identifies and analyzes numbers that appear out of pattern, conducts research of possible causes, and resolves data issues
  • Supports and/or leads development/design of new recording processes and actuarial techniques up to moderate complexity
  • Understands and follows internal and external compliance requirements/standards
  • Understands market specific implications of complex business strategies
  • Trains/coaches team members or peers and actively shares expertise with peers
Job Qualifications
  • Bachelor’s degree required – preferably in related field of study such as actuarial science, math/applied math, statistics/applied statistics, economics
  • 3-5 years of experience, two or more years of property & casualty reserving experience preferred
  • Working knowledge of basic to moderately complex loss reserving techniques
  • Basic understanding of GAAP and Statutory Accounting principles
  • Strong Computer proficiency in Microsoft Office; Advanced – Excel skills
  • Working knowledge of R or similar programming language
  • Statistical modeling experience preferred
  • Strong knowledge in the use of data sources and applications to conduct research
  • Has an understanding of current industry and professional standards/ environment to impact decisions and indicate requirements
  • Possesses intermediate working knowledge of policies and procedures in management or other technical fields
  • Strong written and verbal communication skills including the ability to effectively collaborate with multi-disciplinary groups and all organizational levels
  • High level organizational and project management skills to handle multiple concurrent assignments in a timely manner and to monitor processes throughout a team or department
  • Strong decision making skills
  • Works effectively in a team environment
