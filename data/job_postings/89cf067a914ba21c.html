Web Application Developer, Engineering Data - Fremont, CA

We are looking for a highly skilled Full Stack Developer who will support our vehicle design, manufacturing and data analytics team with web apps to analyze and visualize engineering, production and fleet data. The ideal candidate is comfortable with both front and back end programming, and will be responsible for designing and developing front end web architecture, user interaction and database.
Full Stack Developers will be required to see out a project from conception to final product, requiring good organization skills and attention to details.

Responsibilities for Full Stack Developer
  • Design and develop web apps to analyze and visualize engineering, production and fleet data
  • Maintain and improve existing web apps to support Vehicle Engineering team
  • Create servers and database to manage engineering, production and fleet data

Required Qualifications
  • Degree in Computer Science or Engineering
  • Proficiency with front end languages: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, etc
  • Familiarity with JavaScript frameworks: React, Vue, Angular, etc
  • Familiarity with database technologies: MySQL, PostgreSQL, MongoDB, etc
  • Proficiency with server side languages: Python, Ruby, Java, PHP, etc
  • Experience in developing data visualization front end

Desirable Qualifications
  • Organizational and project management skills
  • Familiarity with web-based 3D visualization using WebGL and three.js
  • Familiarity with JIRA REST API
  • Appetite to diverge to or work with Data Scientist team
  • Knowledge and experience in statistical and data mining techniques: PCA, GLM/Regression, Random Forest, Boosting, etc
  • Experience using statistical computer languages (R, Python, etc.) to manipulate data and draw insights from large data sets
  • Knowledge of a variety of machine learning techniques (clustering, decision tree learning, artificial neural networks, etc.) and their real-world advantages/drawbacks
  • Knowledge of 3D CAD / PLM systems such as CATIA
