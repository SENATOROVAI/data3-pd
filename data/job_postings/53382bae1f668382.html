Systems Administrator - (Exchange and Server Administration) - Vallejo, CA 94598

Under the direction of the Director of Information Technology, the MS Exchange and Server Administrator will manage and maintain campus email systems, server hardware and software, networking infrastructure, Active Directory (AD) and VMware.
The campus Exchange and Server Administrator will ensure reliable operability of the campus email infrastructure, manage and maintain campus server and VMware infrastructure, ensure reliable operability of campus servers, monitor server utilization, make recommendations for preventative maintenance, retire and replace aging servers and network equipment, manage and maintain TUC Active Directory (AD) structure and user accounts, address daily Server Operations trouble tickets and tasks, and provide a leadership role in projects directly relating to email and server services for the TUC campus.

The Exchange and Server Administrator will interact on a daily basis with other members of the Information Technology Department, as well as with faculty, students, staff, and administration. Interaction with network staff on other Touro campuses is direct as this position requires coordination of corresponding services on those campuses.

The Exchange and Server Administrator will also act as a campus IT Project Manager and will coordinate cross-functional roles and responsibilities with colleagues in the Information Technology departments of other Touro campuses as needed. S/he will manage assigned projects following an established TUC PMO methodology for project management and delivery.


Job Duties
  • Responsible for day-to-day administration of campus Exchange services and server operations
  • Responsible for project management of assigned IT operational projects
  • Assist with planning, design, deploy, configure, maintain, and monitor Exchange, appliances, servers, network interfaces, and print sharing devices
  • Administer server and VMware infrastructure services and provide solutions to escalated server issues
  • Act as a liaison with internal and external customers in support of exchange, server, VMware technology needs and requirements
  • Assist with defining and implementing server infrastructure disaster recovery for hardware, software, information protection and file integrity
  • Oversee server security systems, including server monitoring, security audits, intrusion detection, etc
  • Develop, monitor, and report on Exchange, VMware, and network performance metrics
  • Manage and report IT project progress following established TUC PMO methodology for project delivery (Schedule, Budget, & Resources)
