Internship - Data Scientist, Denver CO (Summer 2020) - Denver, CO 80221

Job Description Summary
Internships are short-term work experiences for a designated period of time that allow college students the opportunity to observe and participate in the professional work environment, to explore how his/her interests relate to possible careers, and to gain practical experience in an industry relating to their field of study. What You Will Learn: At Transamerica, we believe achieving a secure future requires both smart financial planning and a healthy lifestyle. We’re using data science, machine learning, computer vision, natural language processing, and IoT to revolutionize the way our customers save, invest, protect, and retire and to help them develop better wellness habits. As part of the Advanced Analytics group, you will work with product managers, data engineers, and other data scientists to develop innovative solutions that transform the way we do business
Job Description

What Success Looks Like & What You’ll Do (Responsibilities):

  • Design experiments and formulate machine learning problems to address major business challenges and innovation opportunities
  • Work with product managers and data engineers to discover, collect, cleanse, and refine the data needed for analysis and modeling
  • Analyze large datasets to extract actionable insights and inform experimental design and model development
  • Write lucid, compelling summaries to communicate results of your analysis, model development, and testing which inform how the business should act
  • Collaborate with peers, challenging assumptions and providing constructive criticism and ideas for improvement
  • Build models using a variety of statistical and machine learning techniques, assessing the best type of model for the problem context and using advanced techniques and customization's to optimize model performance
  • Harden models for production deployment and monitor model performance over time


Who You Are (Qualifications):

  • Pursuing a Bachelors or Masters in computer science, machine learning, data science, mathematics, statistics, or a related quantitative field
  • You have knowledge of major machine learning algorithms
  • You have beginning-intermediate skills in Python and R
  • You have practical experience with machine learning libraries and platforms, like Scikit-learn and Tensorflow
  • Your statistical analysis skills are solid and you have a good understanding of experimental design
  • You have excellent analytical and problem solving skills
  • You can demonstrate your experience working with data at scale in the Hadoop ecosystem
  • You have great communication skills and a particular talent for using data to tell a story
  • You love to learn and keep pace with the latest advances in data science and machine learning
  • Students must be actively pursuing a degree at a college or university and must not graduate prior to completion of the internship
