Global Consumer Knowledge
Marketing Science:
Nike’s Consumer Direct Sciences is a growing team responsible for building and deepening a holistic view of Nike’s consumers through data and analytics and applying those insights to inform the development of incredible content & experiences for our consumers. Part of that holistic view involves having a deep understanding of our consumers and what marketing content and attributes resonate best with them which allows us to combine the science with the art of Nike’s marketing.

Consumer Data Science & Personalization:
The Personalization team in Nike's Global Consumer Knowledge Center of Excellence is a fast-growing team responsible for building and deepening a holistic view of Nike's consumers through data and analytics, and applying those insights to personalize and optimize incredible digital services and experiences for our consumers. As a Consumer Data Science & Personalization Intern at Nike, you will be responsible for helping us build the next generation of personalized experiences that power many of our digital channels delivering direct and unbreakable relationships with hundreds of millions of our customers.

Global Sourcing & Manufacturing
Global Souring & Manufacturing teams dream up new ways to make better-performing products faster and more efficiently. This world-class team challenges assumptions about how our products are made and leads the company in pursuing ambitious sustainability goals — all while producing Nike’s rich mix of product. Manufacturing and Engineering works closely with design teams, categories, manufacturing partners, material suppliers and logistics providers across more than 40 countries on six continents. They navigate complex geopolitics with multiple supply chains and diverse ecosystems while remaining focused on manufacturing, materials, sustainability and product quality.

Marketplace Analytics
Consumer demand is Nike demand. We'll get the right product into the hands of the consumer faster through end-to-end demand and supply management fueled by consistent data and advanced analytics. We'll drive marketplace health and mutual profitability by delivering insights to help our business partners manage supply and inventory investments and drive to a better forecast of demand in the marketplace.

Global Operations & Logistics
Nike’s Operations team ensures that every year over one billion pieces of footwear, apparel and equipment arrive at the right destination on time across the globe. The complex process involves 60+ distribution nodes, thousands of accounts, and over 100,000 retail doors. The team constantly pushes to become faster, more efficient, more reliable, and more flexible, while minimizing its environmental impact, to support changing consumer needs.

Sports Marketing
Global Sports Marketing Analytics develops data-driven insights and tools to inform decision-making in sports marketing. The team’s top three objectives include:
  • Tracking and measuring the sports marketing portfolio and positioning
  • Identifying and exploring opportunities to inform deal making through in-depth analysis

From sourcing data, designing tools, and delivering insights, Global Sports Marketing Analytics sits in the center of the Sports Marketing function and acts as the in-house strategy and analytics team problem-solving for various team needs.
