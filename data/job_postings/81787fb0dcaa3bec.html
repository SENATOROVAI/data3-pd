About this role

BlackRock Financial Management, Inc. seeks Associate, Data Science and Client Analytics in Princeton, NJ to work with US Wealth Advisory and Digital Wealth groups to leverage consumer data collected through the CRM, third party sources, vendors and the website. Req’s: Master’s degree or equivalent in Business Administration, Finance, or a related field and 3 years of experience in job offered or related occupation: utilizing SQL Server and MySQL, and statistical programming languages including R, SAS, SPSS; utilizing web analytics tools including Adobe Omniture and Google Analytics to recommend ROI optimizing strategies from attribution modeling to A/B or multivariate experimentation; analyzing visualizations using Tableau and TIBCO Spotfire; utilizing tree-based models, regression analysis, and hypothesis testing; utilizing web analytics and website optimization to enhance web user experience, increase product visibility, and aid in customer acquisition and retention. In the alternative, employer will accept a Bachelor’s degree or equivalent in one of the above fields and 5 years of experience in the above skills.
