Position: Junior Data Scientist


Scope of position: We are looking for an early-career scientist (recent graduate) to join our advance analytics science team. This position requires someone with a good theoretical knowledge in the field of data science (statistics and machine learning) and computer skills to apply this knowledge in healthcare space.
