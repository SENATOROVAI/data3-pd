Technologist/Foundry Engineer - Sunnyvale, CA 94085

Technologist/Foundry Engineer with a passion for product development to support SOC and Mixed Signal ASIC product development.

Responsibilities

The position will support for new product development on exciting new projects. The will be involved in technology assessment, technology development, product design support and device development support as well as other interesting challenges as we strive to push the limits of our products.
The position requires someone with experience in the Fabless semiconductor model with some experience in custom process and custom library development. Candidate should have a good understanding of device physics, transistor modeling, fabrication processes for a wide range of technology nodes, device reliability, statistical analysis, yield improvement, and physical failure analysis techniques.
