HRSC Specialist IV - Alameda, CA 94502

Under limited supervision, performs a variety of general personnel/clerical tasks in such areas as employee records, benefits, education/training, employment, compensation, and equal employment opportunity. Maintains both manual and automated personnel records, collects and compiles sensitive and confidential personnel statistics and prepares reports. In accordance with procedures, furnishes information to authorized persons and/or agencies. Provides information to all levels of employees regarding personnel policies and procedures.


Essential Functions:


  • At the National Human Resources Services Center (NHRSC), under limited supervision, processes complex Human Resources employee job or benefit related transactions within several of the following Human Resources programs: hire/rehire, job changes including promotion, demotion, lateral moves, pay, termination, benefits including retirement, leaves of absences, disability, worker's compensation according to established policies and procedures, collective bargaining agreements and laws
  • Acts as a resource to Human Resources Professionals, NHRSC Liaisons, Management, and other service center team members. Responds professionally, accurately and timely to employee requests for information and changes by analyzing and processing complex Human Resources transactions pertaining to employee pay, benefits including retirement, leaves, and workers ' compensation and job data changes
  • Retrieves, enters, and processes complex employee related data utilizing Windows-based Human Resources information systems. Responsible for research and resolution of complex escalated transactions received from various sources including but not limited to department email channel, Human Resource Professionals, NHRSC Liaisons, and/or Regional Leaders
  • Analyzes and resolves ongoing, complex Human Resources related issues and maintains accurate employee data utilizing Human Resources information systems
  • Make outbound calls to transaction initiators and may require handling of incoming employee and manager calls as needed
  • Collaborates with Quality Assurance, Learning Services, NHRSC Transactional Management Content Leads, and Team Management to modify transaction procedures as needed and to provide one-on-one training for NHRSC Specialist II and III employees and/or other NHRSC employees
  • Manages transactional workflow distribution for department NHRSC Specialist II&III employees
  • Performs monthly audits for NHRSC Specialist II&III employees to ensure accuracy in support of department quality goals
  • Performs other related duties as required
