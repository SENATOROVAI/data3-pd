Data Analyst II (SQL/ETL/Statistics) - United States

Overview

This intermediate analyst participates in the production of operational, financial, effectiveness and market intelligence data by discovering and querying repositories and other sources. The analyst also interprets data, formulates reports and makes recommendations based upon the research findings. This effort requires the application of qualitative and quantitative techniques to interpret the data and produce substantiated recommendations. At this career level, the incumbent generally works independently on moderately complex business intelligence projects or as a team member on larger projects. He or she may coach other analysts.
