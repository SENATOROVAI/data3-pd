Business Analyst - Medical Claims Data Projects - San Francisco, CA 94104

Title: Project Manager Special Projects Department: Claims

Position Type: Non-Exempt Hours per Week: 40

Position Summary

Responsible for collecting and studying business-related data/ issues. The work can involve evaluating information and identifying relevant data that needs to be stored and analyzed; project management, and or process change. This includes primary engagement in data analysis for the purpose of risk assessment, data interpretation and business decision guidance and process improvement. Uses a variety of computer programs to compile information. They will work collaboratively with other employees/management/business partners

"Has minimum necessary access to Protected Health Information PHI and Personally Identifiable Information PII.

Key Duties and Responsibilities:

Core skills:

  • Highly developed analytical skills
  • Mastery of basic math
  • Knowledge of business operations practices
  • Working knowledge of Microsoft Office Suite programs, especially Excel and PowerPoint
  • Adept at research practices
  • Consults with functional unit management and personnel to identify, define and document business needs and objectives, current operational procedures, problems and input/output requirements
  • Proactively communicates and collaborates with external and internal customers to analyze information needs and functional requirements and deliver the following artifacts as needed: Functional requirements, Business Requirements Document, Client and Client Service Data Requirements
  • Tracks and fully documents changes for functional and business specifications; writes detailed universally understood procedures for permanent records and use in training
  • Identifies opportunities for improving business processes through information systems and/or non-system driver changes; assists in the preparation of proposals to develop new systems and/or operational changes
  • Able to locate collect pertinent information need to perform data analysis
  • Responsible for pulling data and to review the information and use it to draw conclusions that may help them determine ways to change procedures or improve business operations
  • Create detailed reports to be presented to management
  • Create visual depictions of data, graphs and charts and present the data
  • Create presentations using software such as, Microsoft PowerPoint
  • Able to review surveys and obtain documentation
  • Must be able to determine what information is needed and where the best source for that information can be found
  • Successfully engage in multiple initiatives simultaneously
  • Perform other related duties and special projects as assigned
  • Support the mission, vision and values of the company and the team through role modeling and encouraging desired behaviors

Minimum Qualifications

  • Bachelor's degree in a related field, or equivalent combination of education and work experience
  • Minimum of 1 year experience in a medical claim processing environment for medical, dental and/or hospital claims; working knowledge of benefits claims adjudication principles and procedures, medical and/or dental terminology and ICD-9 and CPT-4 codes
  • Knowledgeable on data analysis practices and be comfortable with statistics and mathematical calculations
  • Possess a strong work ethic and be a team player
  • Highly developed sense of integrity and commitment to customer satisfaction
  • Ability to communicate clearly and professionally, both verbally and in writing
  • Ability to function independently within established guidelines
  • Solid organization skills with strong attention to detail
  • Strong decision making, research and analytical abilities
  • Proficient using Microsoft Office, including MS Word and Excel
  • Familiarity of the TPA business and Taft-Hartley Welfare Funds preferred

Working Conditions/Physical Effort

  • Typical office
  • Typically sitting at a desk or table, working on a computer in excess of 85%
  • Travel may be required
