HR Specialist - Oroville, CA 95966

SUMMARY: This position includes working under the supervision of the Director Human Resources, assisting with recruiting and processing of employee, related paperwork, monitors and reports on current staffing levels, maintains job descriptions, posting information, handles status changed and terminated employee information and associated departmental clerical functions. This position entails a combination of public relations and administrative functions, which must be performed with efficiency, diplomacy, confidentiality and a consistently positive attitude.

ESSENTIAL DUTIES:
  • Manages and coordinates multiple tasks and projects in a fast-paced environment
  • Knowledge of employment laws, and governmental compliance as they apply to Native American Gaming
  • Prepares reports, speaks and writes effectively and work under a minimum of direction
  • Exercises good judgment and make sound decisions in a manner consistent with the essential job functions
  • Receive and screen telephone calls, visitors and applicants; answer inquiries regarding job openings, and application procedures
  • Develop and prepare draft job announcements. Posts announcements in Casino and Hotel and on web page. Updates and maintains job hotline and other job posting media
  • Facilitate recruiting and selection process, prepare letters for applicants not chosen for interview; create and maintain accurate records of hiring process
  • Assist departments with scheduling interviews; make arrangements for interviews; schedule rooms; distribute interview information packets; maintain confidentiality of interview process
  • Reviews and prepares paperwork for approval of new hires, termed employees and status change. Maintains reports on current staffing levels
  • Processes new and terminated employees with associated paperwork. Closes out files
  • Responsible for maintaining the highest degree of confidentiality
  • Responsible for covering various duties of other Human Resource staff
  • Be accountable for and fully utilize EDGE, our guest experience program, with each guest interaction
  • Other duties as assigned
