Research Analyst Senior - Roadway Data - Boise, ID 83702

Description

IDAHO TRANSPORTATION DEPARTMENT

Our Mission: Your Safety, Your Mobility, Your Economic Opportunity

Are you interested in working for an award-winning, dynamic, professional state agency? This opening may be your opportunity to use your education, knowledge and skills to promote the mission of Idaho Transportation Department (ITD).

ITD is hiring a Senior Research Analyst located at our Headquarters office in Boise, Idaho. This position will be working for the Division of Engineering Services in our Roadway Data section. The incumbent in this position will collect, analyze and report federally mandated data in a timely and conscientious manner. The incumbent will also monitor and analyze data, as well as interpret potential hardware-related issues for continuous count devices

Example of Duties

  • Polls data from continuous count sites;
  • Develops research methodology, procedures, and tools for data collection, maintenance, and reporting;
  • Develops quality control techniques;
  • Creates ad hoc reports from continuous count data;
  • Analyzes information systems and recommends modifications;
  • Edits, modifies and compiles statistical data from manual count and computer programs; may confer with computer personnel to develop programs;
  • Analyzes, updates, and reviews data to determine statistical accuracy of data files and reports;
  • Interprets trends and results of studies;
  • Develops conclusions, makes recommendations, and drafts report of findings into appropriate format for various users;
  • Establish methods and procedures to assure quality of data;
  • Act as a representative on research teams and/or committees;
  • Work with outside agencies such as COMPASS, FHWA, and local highway districts;
  • Research as necessary to provide information to management and the state legislature;
  • Manage traffic software requirements to meet any internal or external needs;
  • Design and implement traffic data models

Minimum Qualifications

Good knowledge of:

  • Research methods;
  • Principles of statistics;
  • Sampling theory and probability theory
Experience:
  • Writing analytical (research) reports or materials;
  • Compiling and analyzing statistical data and drawing conclusions;
  • Using statistical software or spreadsheet software to perform statistical analysis;
  • Interpreting and communicating (orally and in writing) statistical material into information usable by a target audience
DESIRED QUALIFICATION:
  • Work experience or education using Geospatial Information System tools, such as ArcMap, ArcGIS Online, and Others;
  • Work experience or education troubleshooting software or hardware;
  • Work experience or education with SQL
