Sales Executive - San Jose, CA

Job Summary Plans, administers, controls and organizes the activities of a label printing and converting production sales territory to meet and exceed profitable sales and earnings objectives. Develops and implements territory selling plans with existing and new customers in the assigned markets. Consistently achieves sales goals and builds value in the market with key customers. Essential Functions These are functions a qualified incumbent or applicant must be able to perform with or without reasonable accommodation. Territory Sales:
  • Achieve annual territory revenue and margin plans. Converts targeted accounts to ITW Labels while growing revenues with existing account. Penetrates prospects by cold calls, telephone contact, referrals and any possible method of creating an opportunity for making a sales presentation
  • Recommends pricing and promotional programs with adequate support data to manager. Quotes pricing to customers within the guidelines provided by the Director/VP of Sales
  • Prospects and generates leads for new business opportunities
  • Develops plans to grow business with existing accounts and executes those plans
    • Communication and Organization:

  • Participate on planning and capabilities assessment team as needed. May assist in resolving customer complaint issues by interfacing with customers and support team
  • Utilize and update CRM system with all customer and prospect activity
  • Addresses ‘non-payment' or ‘late payment' issues with customers
  • Facilitates generation of proper documents as requested by customers
  • Follows all department work instructions
    • Leadership:

  • Positive attitude. Builds rapport with internal and external stakeholders
  • Proactively provides feedback and recommendations to manager. Works with Customer Service and Production to develop appropraite strategies in territory
  • Reporting responsibilities could include Weekly activity reports, monthly sales reports, call documentation, and competitive activities (price, products & other)
