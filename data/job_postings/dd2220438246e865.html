Sr Data Analyst - New York, NY 10022

Position Summary

Discovery is looking for a Senior Data Analyst with a strong technical background to support data product innovation and process automation in a data-driven advertising sales team. Reporting to the Data Science Lead, this individual would use analytics and data engineering to drive business efficiencies and advance the development and enhancement of data products.

Responsibilities

Data Engineering
1. Build data pipelines and batch processes
2. Build complex SQL queries on disparate databases and reporting systems
3. Python scripting for data prep, cleaning, and validation

Data Product / Solutions Development
1. Work with a cross-functional team of data professionals and advertising sales people to define and test requirements for enhancing audience targeting systems and processes
2. Build proofs of concept and prototypes
3. Manage/participate in scrum projects and sprints

Other
1. Support various data cleansing and modeling needs of Data Science Lead
2. Support ad hoc requests from internal Sales team that require automation of data processes or other technical solutions (small and medium tasks)
3. Data visualizations and dashboarding using various data viz tools (Tableau, matplotlib, ggplot2, shiny apps, etc.)
4. Data and process documentation
5. Interfacing with internal IT and Cloud Engineering teams on various issues
