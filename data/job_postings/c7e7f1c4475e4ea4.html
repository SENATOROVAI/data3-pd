Lead Platform Engineer - Machine Learning - Lake Buena Vista, FL

The Lead Machine Learning Platform Engineer spearheads development and operationalization of key framework components required in the end-to-end machine leaning lifecycle. The platform enables multiple studios of developers, data engineers, data scientists, content creators and business analysts across the entire Disney Parks Experiences and Products (DPEP) organization to build, deploy, sustain and continuously improve AI services.
