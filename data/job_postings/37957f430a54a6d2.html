The Role: As a Senior Data Analyst for our Core Products team, you will help to build and improve upon TRON's revenue and user engagement initiatives. You will work alongside a Data Engineering team which manages ETL infrastructure, both planning the creation of new metrics and converting data from those metrics into actionable insights in support of product team initiatives. Your work will directly impact the product and business decisions across the product lifecycle. You will:
  • Support the planning and impact assessment for product and growth initiatives
  • Champion forecasting methodologies needed to generate revenue and product usage forecasts
  • Communicate a holistic picture of product and business performance
  • Monitor data quality, investigate data issues, and ensure issues are identified, documented, tracked, reported and resolved (with product and data engineering)
  • Develop strong relationships with the cross-functional team, and independently provide insight related to releases and other work the team is completing
  • Investigate anomalies in business metrics to identify and correct data quality issues or drive corrective action from the product team in a timely manner

  • Creativity and enthusiasm about finding new solutions and using data to develop new approaches to problems
  • Exceptional problem-solving, communication, and presentation skills (must have the ability to communicate complex quantitative analysis in a clear, precise and actionable manner)
  • Excellent interpersonal skills, be a self-starter and be able to thrive in a cross-functional team
