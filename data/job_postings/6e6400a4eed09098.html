Business Intelligence Analyst - Irvine, CA

Ingram Micro touches 80% of the technology you use every day with our focus on Technology Solutions, Cloud, and Commerce and Lifecycle Solutions. With $46 billion in revenue, we have become the world’s largest technology distributor with operations in 56 countries and more than 30,000 associates. We continue to strategically expand our global reach with 32 acquisitions since 2012.

Position Summary:

Develops and analyzes data models that generate knowledge into the project owner’s business objectives. Utilizes reports of findings and creates appropriate materials to communicate the findings. He or she will maintain a broad understanding of Ingram Micro’s business model along with becoming educated on particular vendors, customers, or internal division’s business models and needs.

Responsibilities:

Major Responsibility: Analysis and Reporting
Candidate must be able to pull data from multiple sources and have familiarity with relational databases. Must understand client’s objectives and translate business needs into the appropriate technical data models. Responsible for ad-hoc analysis and reporting, including presenting findings to internal clients. Analysis includes trend identification, opportunity assessment and forecasting. Must be able to apply results of data models to business demands.

Requires familiarity with at least 2 of the following:

  • Query Languages (SQL, Python)
  • Automation (SQL Stored Procedures, SSIS, APIs)
  • Reporting/Data Visualization Tools (Excel, Microstrategy, Power BI, etc.)
  • Modeling Techniques (linear regression, logistic regression, decision

Provide opportunity assessments, program ROI and ad-hoc reporting for multiple business units, departments and projects.

Major Responsibility: Project Automation

Work closely with internal/external clients, data science and various teams to understand business/project objectives and provide recommendations based on supporting analysis. Transform data analytics to actionable business applications. Responsible for accountability toward project status and client deliverables

Major Responsibility: Communication

Effectively listen, communicate, recommend, and present data analytic solutions to internal and external clients. Must have ability to communicate analytic concepts to a non-technical audience. This includes over the phone and in person presentations with PowerPoint and other media sources.

Develop, maintain and prepare content including ROI reporting and opportunity assessments.

Education:
  • Master’s degree in quantitative discipline (Statistics, Applied Mathematics, Operations Research, Computer Science, or Econometrics) and 1 year of experience OR Bachelor’s degree in quantitative field with 3-5 years relevant experience
  • Experience in analysis of large data sets, identifying trends and patterns, and effectively communicating both internally and externally
  • Requires familiarity with at least 2 of the following:
    • Query Languages (SQL, Python)
    • Automation (SQL Stored Procedures, SSIS, APIs)
    • Reporting/Data Visualization Tools (Excel, Microstrategy, Power BI, etc.)
    • Modeling Techniques (linear regression, logistic regression, decision tree)
  • Must have managed at least one project in which a relational database was built and utilized for analysis
