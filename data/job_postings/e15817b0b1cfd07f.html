Principal Statistician - Los Angeles, CA

Responsibilities

Individual to work on health related medical research projects in a wide variety of areas. Individual to provide data base construction, data management, statistical analysis and advanced statistical modeling for Dr. Joann Elmore in the Department of Medicine in the Division of General Internal Medicine & Health Services Research. Primary job duties include collaborating on study design, data quality control, statistical modeling, designing study databases, data management and conducting statistical analyses. Doctoral degree in Biostatistics or Statistics. Computer competence using statistical software (SAS and Splus/R), spreadsheet programs, and graphing software required.

Qualifications

Knowledge of basic and multivariate statistics. Knowledge of research design. Skill in statistical software applications, notably SAS and Splus/R. Ability to conceptualize desired tables and graphs and create them from raw data. Ability to write manuscripts for scientific peer-review journals. Ability to recognize, spell, and use statistical and medical terminology appropriately. Ability to summarize results of statistical analyses for medical collaborators and for peer-reviewed publication. Ability to use software, Microsoft Word, PowerPoint, and Excel. Skill in editing for spelling, punctuation, and grammar. Ability to select and pass on relevant information to co-investigators. Knowledge of basic computer hardware. Skill in speaking clearly and distinctively using appropriate vocabulary and grammar to obtain and convey information within and outside the University. Ability to perform duties and tasks independently with minimal supervision. Demonstrated ability to establish and maintain cooperative working relationships with other staff members and faculty. Experience reading and interpreting study protocols. Experience with advanced statistical techniques including ordinal regression and zero inflated negative binomial models.
