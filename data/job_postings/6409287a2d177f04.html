Role: ----- As the Deal Desk Analyst, you will partner with the sales team to define and support standard/complex deal procedures, while attaining approval from the necessary decision-makers. You will be a critical member of the sales team--helping us become a data-led, metrics-driven organization. Deliverables include all aspects of deal structuring; pricing, quote creation, contract reviews and non-standard terms working closely with Linqia's sales team to ensure deals are managed to their conclusion. The ideal candidate is scrappy, proactive, creative, assertive, deeply analytical and detailed-oriented, but capable of using structured thinking to cut through ambiguity. This is a hands-on role that requires heavy communication and data analysis in a constantly changing, fast-paced environment. You are eager to find answers and be a part of scaling an organization from the ground up. Responsibilities: -----------------
  • Partner with our sales, customer success, finance, and legal teams to define standard / non-standard deal criteria and related approval processes
  • Manage the day-to-day of complex non-standard/standard deals and serve as the primary point of contact for sales, finance, and legal in these transactions
  • Provide insights to the sales team on deal structuring and best practices
  • Craft competitive, healthy deal strategies that optimize the sales cycle and lead to an increased win rate
  • Ensure opportunities are properly created and tracked in Salesforce including stage, probability, deal size, and various other required fields
  • Deliver regular updates to sales management for deals in the deal review process
  • Identify and drive the implementation of new tools and process improvements to support sales growth

  • 2+ years of relevant experience in a similar role

  • Experience working in sales operations function with similar needs and job function
  • Strong working knowledge and expertise in spreadsheets, Salesforce, and presentation software
  • Excellent verbal and written communication skills
  • Comfort working in ambiguity with a bent towards fast execution and iteration
  • Project management and ability to stay on task
  • BA/BS degree or equivalent
