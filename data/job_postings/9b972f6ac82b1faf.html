Science and Engineering Associate - Sample Delivery [Regular Fixed Term] - Menlo Park, CA

SLAC National Accelerator Laboratory (SLAC) is one of the world’s premier research laboratories, providing an internationally leading capability in the form of its accelerator based light sources and its science programs. The Linac Coherent Light Source (LCLS), a directorate of SLAC and the world’s first hard x-ray free electron laser, seeks a Science and Engineering Associate with a background in sample introduction for serial data collection to join the LCLS Technical Support Group. The Technical Support Group is responsible for providing installation and maintenance for various instrumentation and components such as motors, cameras, vacuum, power supplies, temperature control, x-ray cameras and waveform digitizers in support of the X-Ray infrastructure Users operations, and operations involving research and experimental activities. Our Technical Support team is also responsible for the Electrical Equipment Safety Inspection Program as well as the design, deployment and maintenance for Laser Safety Systems across the entire SLAC complex. In this position, you will primarily support our Sample Environment & Delivery (SED) group with sample preparation, characterization and delivery and the development and integration of methods for sample introduction. You will assist with automation of sample introduction at the LCLS beam lines and will support the user experiment program. You will also have the opportunity to collaborate with researchers at SLAC and Stanford University working on simulations and theoretical frameworks of the underlying physics. The successful candidate should be curious, motivated and self-driven.
