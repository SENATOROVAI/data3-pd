2020 INTERNSHIP PROGRAM OVERVIEW

Interns are paid hourly a minimum of $19/hr based on position and level of education. Our benefits include travel to and from the internship paid for up front by adidas as well as an additional stipend to assist in your time in Portland, OR.

The Internship program includes the following elements to round out the work you do with our design teams on a day to day basis:

  • Intern Orientation
  • Speaker Series with business leadership
  • Cross-Functional Project
  • Social Events
  • Mentorship Program
  • Employee Perks – product discount, gym membership, etc

YOUR TASKS

  • Work as an integral member of the Future Sport Science team to deliver new innovative, commercially viable concepts and products
  • Collaborate with the Future team to build new perception testing guidelines and standards as it relates to performance product
  • Assist team in the testing and engineering of new concepts to shape the future of sport
  • Track and record data related to research and testing of concepts
  • Gather relevant trend and consumer information for the product category
  • KNOWLEDGE SKILLS AND ABILITIES
  • Passion for technical aspects of sport and desire to apply scientific methods to sports products
  • Trend / Industry / consumer insights
  • Proficient in systems for data collection, analysis and presentation (Vicon, Matlab, dartfish)
  • Storytelling - Visual/Verbal Communication
  • Footwear / Apparel / Color & Material knowledge
  • Experience in prototyping, stimulation, technical testing

QUALIFICATIONS

  • Currently enrolled in a 2019-2020 full-time academic program
  • Graduating in winter 2020 or spring 2021
  • Eligible to work in the US without restrictions
  • Available to work full-time in Portland, Oregon for the duration of the 12-week program (May 27th – August 14th) ***June start date optional based on academic calendar***
  • Submit a portfolio of your work (optional)
