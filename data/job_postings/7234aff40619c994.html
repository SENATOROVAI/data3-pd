Department Overview

The men and women of Electric Operations ensure the delivery of safe and reliable electric service to our customers. Electric Operations is responsible for every aspect of PG&E’s electric distribution and transmission operations, including planning, engineering, maintenance and construction, asset management, business planning, restoration and emergency response.


Position Summary

In Transmission Operations, the capital work portfolio consists mainly of multi-year projects. The Analyst Capital Work Readiness Lead is responsible for providing transparency for both Transmission Operations and Electric Business Operations related to our readiness to execute our work, including reporting, line-of-business action plan review processes, and related day-to-day interactions with personnel responsible for both planning and executing the work.


Job Responsibilities

  • Communicates with peers and management inside and outside of the department exchanging ideas or gathering information
  • Determines data needs, collects data, selects from different analytical techniques to conduct root cause analysis and determine issues
  • May participate on project teams
  • Understands business process needs and develops new reports, performs custom and ad-hoc reports working independently. Presents findings to various audiences
  • Monthly reporting for TO Work Plan Review (WPR) on key units, executability and construction readiness and ad hoc analysis
  • Administrative duties for Transmission Operations (TO) partner coordination call, Capital to Investment Execution (CIE) forum and process improvement workstreams
  • Backup reporting for integrated change request routing and associated reporting
