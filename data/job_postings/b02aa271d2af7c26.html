Data Analytics Intern - Los Angeles, CA 90015

BI Intern will join our Business Insights group to provide analysis, predict performance, and research trends using data from all departments of LA Clippers Business Operations. As BI Intern, you will participate in projects both long and short term that provide tools and insights for Marketing, Ticket Sales, Partnerships, and other departments as needed. Ideal candidates will be in school or a recent graduate with some experience (school projects OK) in data visualization, statistical software and scripting languages. To be considered for this role, you should be available for part-time work on a short term (6-9 month) basis. The job will have flexible hours to work around existing or future class schedule.

This is a part-time role based in our DTLA office. It pays $14.25 per hour, with a workload of 16-24 hours per week. This role is not benefit-eligible.

Interview Process will culminate with a Hackathon on Friday, November 15th at our office in DTLA. Hackathon will be invitation only and invites will be extended after the first round of interviews. Finalists must be available to participate in the Hackathon so please hold that time.

Principal Duties and Responsibilities

  • Manipulate, visualize, and analyze large data sets
  • Design, improve, and maintain ongoing metrics, reports, analyses, dashboards, etc
  • Design, construct, install, test and maintain data management systems
  • Align architecture with business requirements
  • Develop data set processes for data mining, modeling, and production
  • Employ an array of languages and tools to connect systems together
  • Recommend different ways to constantly improve data reliability, efficiency, and quality
  • Prepare data for predictive and prescriptive modeling
  • Support gathering of additional data for ad-hoc analysis as needed
  • Collaborate effectively on team projects and work autonomously on individual work as appropriate
  • Communicate results to the Business Operations staff through presentations, written reports, and tools

Characteristics / Qualifications

  • Experience with SQL Databases & Database Management
  • Statistical expertise and experience with statistical software (R, SciPy, TensorFlow, …)
  • Experience building and optimizing data pipelines, architectures, and data sets
  • Familiarity with relevant APIs a plus (StubHub API, Facebook Marketing API, Google Ads API, etc.)
  • Experience with varied programming and scripting languages (Python, C++, …)
  • Able to work and excel in high-demands environments and meet hard deadlines
  • Effective interpersonal skills, both oral and written
  • Self-starter and entrepreneurial spirit with hands-on approach towards business
