Machine Learning Engineer - Bethesda, MD 20817

We are looking for a highly motivated machine learning engineer or data scientist to take over as a valuable member of the Corporate HR Data Science team at Lockheed Martin. This position will be responsible for the development and deployment of cutting-edge machine learning and data science solutions to a wide range of opportunities in the people analytics space. As a core member of the data science team, you will play a critical role in the growth of a highly-optimized, data science and machine learning team.

Responsibilities will include:
  • Develop and deploy machine learning services for use in production environments
  • Develop processes and best practices related to libraries used by the team
  • Work with the data science team to assist in the creation and optimization of the team’s production-grade machine learning workflows
  • Explore and recommend emerging technologies and techniques to support/enhance products
Basic Qualifications:
  • Bachelor’s Degree in Computer Science, Data Science, Engineering, or any other technology related field
  • 9 years of professional experience in building scalable, machine learning services (both supervised and unsupervised algorithms)
  • Expertise in one or more languages, preferably Python, but others as well
  • Experience with text embedding representations such as Word2vec, Doc2vec, fastText, BERT, ELMo, XLNet, etc
  • Experience using NLP libraries/models such as spaCy, gensim, flair, etc
  • Experience using machine learning libraries and platforms, such as Tensorflow, Keras, PyTorch, Caffee, Theano, Scikit-Learn, etc
  • Knowledge of machine learning and data science domain and associated model families, such as neural networks, decision trees, Bayesian models, and deep learning algorithms
  • You have experience translating a business problem into a set of executable steps using machine learning techniques
  • Focused attention to process improvement and efficiency
  • Ability to adjust to changing demands; must be able to adjust priorities, working on both urgent requests and longer-term projects
  • Project/plan management; time management and prioritization skills
  • Ability to work across organizations, geographies, levels and to develop and maintain positive professional relationships
  • Communicate verbally and visually in ways the customer understands
  • Demonstrate adaptable consulting and facilitation skills
Desired Skills:
  • Master’s or experience in computer science or other technical related fields
  • Experience managing multiple projects at a time while managing ambiguity
  • Passion for working in a high-paced environment focused on delivering the highest quality products and services to customers
  • Familiarity with cloud services as applied in the DoD and aerospace industry
  • Knowledge of emerging technical skills, tools, and best practices
