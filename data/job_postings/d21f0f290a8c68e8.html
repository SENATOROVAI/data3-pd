ENGINEERING TECHINICIAN - Barstow, CA

Duties

Summary

You will serve as a ENGINEERING TECHNICIAN in the BUSINESS OPERATIONS, ENGINEERING DIVISION of MARINE CORPS LOGISTICS BASE ALBANY.

***This Job Opportunity Announcement is using an additional online assessment tool to evaluate applicants. Please review the HOW YOU WILL BE EVALUATED, HOW TO APPLY, and WHAT TO EXPECT NEXT sections below for instructions.***


Responsibilities

  • You will develop design improvements or modifications of plant tools and equipment to increase both versatility and utilization
  • You will develop recommendations for renovation, for additions to existing structures, or plans for new structures required to maintain the most effective/efficient capability to accomplish the current and projected workload
  • You will have extensive knowledge of waste reduction and hazardous waste control principles and practices
  • You will have knowledge of management techniques including economic analysis, budgeting and expenditure tracking
  • You must have experience in reading and interpreting mechanical drawings and electrical/electronic schematics

    Qualifications

    Your resume must demonstrate at least one year of Specialized experience at or equivalent to the GS-09 grade level or pay band in the Federal service or equivalent experience in the private or public sector. Specialized experience must demonstrate the following: Ability to determine the efficiency, effect, reliability and safety of the systems and equipment repaired, manufactured, or installed improvement: prepare plans, specifications, and engineering estimates for improvements for mechanical equipment and/or facilities as well as review work in progress to ensure conformance with approved specifications to support the organizational mission.

    Your resume must demonstrate at least one year of Specialized experience at or equivalent to the GS-07 grade level or pay band in the Federal service or equivalent experience in the private or public sector. Specialized experience must demonstrate the following: Applying analytical and evaluative techniques utilized to conduct surveys.
