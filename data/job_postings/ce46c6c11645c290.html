Customer Success Manager - Walnut Creek, CA

The Customer Success Manager is a crucial team member at Seal Software. You will work directly with your set of customers to understand and mold the vision for Seal, creating and evolving a mutual plan for achieving that success. Seal knows the contract renewal is the key indicator of success long term, and that is the primary responsibility for the Customer Success Managers. The CSM is ultimately accountable for measured by the renewal and growth of their customers. Responsibilities:
  • Own post-sale customer experience
  • Grow and expand exposure at Seal customers
  • Account planning and strategy
  • Manage cadence of touch points with customers including Steering Committees, QBRs, and ad hoc project work
  • Create metrics and dashboards to monitor client status for both internal and external visibility
  • Defend existing revenue through renewals
    • Requirements
  • 5+ years experience in customer focused roles in customer success, consulting or account management
  • Customer facing experience working across all levels from end-users and department managers, through to VP level or above
  • Proven experience of developing and maintaining multithreaded, complex customer engagements
  • Experience with full post-sale life cycle (implementation, enablement, BAU)
  • Cross functional team leadership experience with internal teams (PS, Sales, Product Development, Technical Support)
  • World class communication skills: C-suite ready
  • Ability to travel up to 50% throughout your territory
