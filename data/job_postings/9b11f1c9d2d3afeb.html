Associate Computational Biologist - Menlo Park, CA

CULTURE:

We’re committed to making a difference for patients. Our focused, creative, and ambitious team makes 3T Biosciences the perfect engine to drive therapeutic solutions to reality. We’re looking for enthusiastic and self-motivated individuals to bring their talents to a fast-paced environment.


TYPICAL RESPONSIBILITIES:

  • Processing next-generation sequencing data, such as RNA sequencing

  • Developing and applying algorithms such as clustering, principal component analysis and others to biological data such as gene expression information and immune repertoire data

  • Working with Amazon Web Services to process data

  • Querying and organizing an SQL database

  • Working with a team of consultants, data scientists, and computational biologists

REQUIRED QUALIFICATIONS:

  • Bachelor’s degree

  • 1-2 years of experience working in a bioinformatics or related field

  • Experience in language-based coding (e.g. python, R, shell scripting, etc.)

  • Experience in using packages to process next-generation sequencing information

  • Self-motivated and a proactive thinker – can work independently and in teams

  • Excellent written and communication skills presenting scientific data


PREFERRED QUALIFICATIONS:

  • MS in bioinformatics or computational biology

  • Experience building and/or using SQL databases

  • Experience in using Amazon Web Services or related cloud services

  • Knowledge in any of the following fields: cellular and molecular biology, structural biology, immunology, protein engineering

  • Familiarity with public databases (e.g. TCGA, Human Protein Atlas, GTEx, etc.)
