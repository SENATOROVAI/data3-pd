Data Center Engineer 2 - Sunnyvale - Sunnyvale, CA

Job Description

We are seeking a Data Center Reliability Engineer located with our team in Sunnyvale, CA. In this position, you will possess a willingness to learn and have a strong can-do attitude. You will work in a team atmosphere and partner of other more experienced Reliability Engineers. Your focus will be primarily on core activities such as reviewing and analyzing data and producing various model simulations.

Your role and responsibilities:

  • Develop reliability block diagrams and perform Monte Carlo simulation via software RELEX (PTC Windchill Quality Solutions)
  • Perform basic reliability analysis in the design phase, the analysis includes:
  • Field data collection and failure data analysis
  • Reliability Prediction Analysis
  • Weibull Life Data Analysis
  • Collaborate with a multi-functional group on FMEA / FMECA (Failure Mode, Effect Analysis) for the electrical power distribution and mechanical cooling systems
  • Collaborate with operations team on the CMMS or MAXIMO input data quality and maintain the CMMS/MAXIMO reliability reporting quality
  • Perform statistical probability analysis
Qualifications

Your experience and qualifications:

  • Strong attention to detail
  • Reliability Engineering background or knowledge
  • Exposure or experience of software RELEX or Windchill Quality Solution
  • Self-starter with the ability to work as an individual contributor and to contribute as part of a larger team
  • Knowledge of electrical engineering (power) and/or mechanical engineering (HVAC) experience
  • 3-5 years of facility operation or electrical/mechanical engineering background
  • Associates or B.S. in Electrical Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Reliability Engineering, or a related technical field

Preferred (but not required) experience and qualifications:

  • Mechanical Cooling system knowledge or experience
  • Data Center Facility Maintenance and Operations experience
  • Power/Water Utility experience
