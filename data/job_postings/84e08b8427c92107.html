Director of Product Development and Manufacturing - Berkeley, CA

About us

At the forefront of farming and food, Memphis Meats is innovating new ways to produce real meat, without the need to breed, feed and slaughter animals. Our process begins with an animal cell, which is cultivated and harvested as meat. Memphis Meats is a mission-driven company focused on bringing to meat lovers an authentic product that is truly humane, sustainably produced, nutritious and delicious! We are now expanding our team of dedicated professionals across disciplines to make better meat for a better world.

About this Job

The Director of Product Development and Manufacturing will play a key role in establishing, defining, and enforcing key meat food production, processing, and packaging processes required to ensure safe and delicious meat food products for consumers at pilot and commercial scale. This role may be tasked with multiple and diverse projects that center around qualifying bench-scale prototypes for pilot scale development as well as establishing and managing the pilot scale food production system.

The Director of Product Development and Manufacturing will be responsible for ensuring appropriate risk-based methods are designed into quality control systems during production, including the successful execution of a Hazard Analysis and Critical Control Point (HACCP) plan, current good manufacturing practices (cGMPs), sanitation standard operations procedures (SSOPs) all in partnership and compliance with federal regulation. This role will lead and manage the documentation and production of cell-based meat product, conceptual diligence in pilot plant processing design and execution, evidence-based methodology consistent with modern food production and processing paradigms under priorities established by leadership. In addition, the Director of Product Development [and Manufacturing] will serve as a thought leader and present results in regular team meetings as well as secure and manage key relationships with external parties as appropriate. The ideal candidate is eager to take on new challenges, is not afraid to fail and is willing to identify mistakes and learn from them.

Duties Include

  • Management and strategic leadership of bench-to-scale-up processes for meat food products including pilot plant design and equipment sourcing
  • Lead daily operation of the food product research and development team
  • Federal, State, and Municipality regulations, compliance, and record-keeping requirements
  • Establishment and adherence to a food safety plan that ensures the highest level of consumer and staff safety
  • Establishment and adherence to a quality management system for meat food products
  • Develop and implement an effective training program for process development staff, on equipment operations, data collections and records, and process assays methods and sampling
  • Drafting Risk Appraisals, Tolerability and acceptability judgements, and risk management plans
  • Drafting appropriate support documentation for federal inspection, safety, and labeling requirements
  • Developing appropriate company production-related risk and safety policy in an evidence-based manner
  • Offering safety plan proposals based upon evidence, regulatory policy, and company goals
  • Effective communication and management of key relationships with external partners and parties, as needed
  • Deploying effective record retention programs consistent with industry best practices for R&D settings as well as scaled production environments
  • Staff training in hazard control and risk prevention
