Predictive Modeler Intern - South Sioux City, NE 68776

As a Predictive Modeler Intern, you will assist the members of the Data & Analytics Department on predictive and descriptive analytics projects, including but not limited to predictive models, dashboards, and general data analysis. You will have exposure to and work with executive level managers and employees across the company, and see the results of your work have a tangible impact on the company.

In order to gain the best experience possible, we need this Predictive Modeler Intern to work full time during the summer of 2020 and recommend he/she continue to work part-time into the 2020-2021 school year.
Qualifications:
  • Bachelor's Degree required, plus must show evidence of enrollment in a graduate program working on a degree preferably in statistics or data science
  • Completion of probability and statistics course required
  • Experience with some statistical programming
  • Work experience that indicates reliability, dependability, and the ability to take direction and work well with others
  • Interpersonal skills with the ability to interact effectively with a variety of individuals
  • Self-initiative in organizing work and following through with assigned tasks
Who we are:
Great West Casualty Company provides specialized insurance products unique to the trucking industry and outstanding customer service to the thousands of truck drivers and trucking companies we serve. Over the past 60 years, we have grown to five offices serving insureds in over 40 states. We are now one of America's largest insurers of trucking companies. If your passion is to help others, you value education and continuous improvement, you enjoy participating in community activities, and you want to be valued for your contributions, come be part of our successful team.
