Job summary

The Red Hat Artificial Intelligence (AI) Center of Excellence (CoE) team is looking for a Data Science Intern to join us in Boston, MA. In this role, you will contribute to our understanding and modeling of product logs, system diagnostic data, product stack, and product quality data. As a subscription-based company, Red Hat must identify drivers of product quality and create models that guide areas of investment to optimize the product packaging and development pipeline. Using machine learning (ML) techniques, you will process large amounts of product and infrastructure log data to create new models using R, Python, Spark, Elasticsearch, Kafka, and more.
