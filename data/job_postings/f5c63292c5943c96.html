Job Summary

Essential Functions

  • Develop and lead quality initiatives to meet operational and business needs in a contact center environment
  • Lead strategic planning activities for Quality and evaluate the potential and practicality of products in development to assure quality and reliability of results
  • Manage and participate in projects including planning, design, development, testing, training, and implementation phases. Projects may include new communication initiatives, system or product enhancements, root cause analysis, and workflow redesign
  • Determine, implement and maintain the most effective method to measure quality among various levels throughout the department, including reporting, trending, and collaboration within the department to recommend process improvements or other changes
  • Evaluate and coordinate regular review of policy and procedure changes and manual design to improve the quality of the department, to include existing documentation, guidelines and materials. Evaluate and report on effectiveness of the changes
  • Define and execute business goals for the team, including but not limited to the establishment of performance standards, metrics, and reporting
  • Work with the entire organization to ensure alignment with organizational Business Strategy, and calibration of evaluative scoring of calls
  • Develop and evaluate staff to ensure the efficient operation of the department
  • Collaborate, coordinate, and communicate across disciplines and departments
  • Demonstrate Company’s Business Strategy and values held within

Requirements

  • Bachelor’s degree in business marketing or related field or experiential equivalent
  • Minimum 7 years experience within a quality assurance role
  • At least 4 years in a leadership role with people management responsibilities
  • Previous experience with managing offshore BPO relationships with an emphasis on evaluating and coordinating regular QA calibrations to ensure consistency with a high confidence interval
  • Experience with quality management tools, such as call monitoring software, preferably Verint (or comparable solution) and customer experience analytics preferred
  • Exceptional verbal and written communication, interpersonal, organizational and time management skills are critical in this role
  • Ability to manage staff in multiple locations
  • Ability to lead cultural change while enhancing performance within a multi-department environment
  • Ability to use software, hardware, and peripherals related to job responsibilities, including MS Office
  • Ability to travel intermittently
  • Project management experience and/or certification preferred
  • Knowledge of Company data systems preferred
