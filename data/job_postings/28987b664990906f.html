Master Scheduler - Wind Projects - Schenectady, NY 12345

Role Summary:

The Master Scheduler is responsible for accurately predicting the short- and Long-term labor demand and labor supply for Wind North America Projects and Services. Results are to be reported on a weekly basis to key stakeholders. Attend labor discussion meetings for Projects and for multiple streams of the Services side of the business. These Services segments include Transactional, Warranty, O&M, Top Issues, Maintenance, and CM&U.


Essential Responsibilities:

As the Master Scheduler - Wind Projects, you will:
  • Analyzes business needs and develops simplified and digitized solutions to improve visibility in forecasting and resource management
  • Create and maintains new staffing mob sheet and contractor reports. Streamlining labor requests to contractors and their responses
  • Flags request that are not filled in a timely fashion for GE review with the contractor company
  • Create and updates EAC page 2 report, which displays comprehensive productivity data, project milestone dates and financial data
  • Create and maintains macros for dashboard and reports that display financial and project milestone analysis
  • Report on changes in portfolio and individual projects to management
  • Analyze staffing level changes and pricing to determine the effectiveness of productivity programs based on given assumptions
  • Create contractor technician maps and demand forecasts (Services/Projects) for contractor company negotiations
  • Collaborate with contractors, resource management, and the project management teams to plan current staffing supply levels for projects and forecast hiring needs
  • Collaborate with GE management in the assignment of contractors to projects
  • Update contractor assignments in project tools
  • Develop and maintains pole sheets. Enables tracking of contractor companies’ technicians and technician’s skill levels
  • Point person for contractor company training on pole sheets and for working on changes to pole sheets
  • Project Management (used by other department such as Logistics, Project Engineering, Contract Management, Quality Assurance, Customer Service)
  • Create project plans using customer schedules, salesforce, and collaboration with project management
  • Update EAC generation tool for creating new project plans and updates templates for project EACs
  • Update project reports as business’s needs change by adjusting focus of the report and analysis
  • Maintain macros that create project reports
  • Calculate and maintains confidence interval and probabilities for project reports
  • Report also forecast services techs and post COD support labor (Services)
  • Track and update projects in Microsoft Project
  • Forecasting unit milestones with feedback from site management
  • Create generator kit order lists for ordering generators
  • Maintain Generator Kit Master Plan
  • Maintain Generator Kit Master Business Plan Smartsheet
  • Maintain map of Repower locations with future generator retrofit work
  • High Voltage maintenance and labor tracking tool (High Voltage team, Services)
  • 3.0 SAM report (Project Management)
  • Delivery certification report (Project Management)
  • CI Budget generation tool (Project Management)
  • Staff Allocations and bonus program (Project Management)
  • Workflow requests Analysis (Services)
  • Gas Meter Service tracker (Services)
  • Company specific commissioner supply reports (Project Management)
  • As needed analysis related to time periods specific growth, location specific project activity, and configuration specific project activity (Product Management, Project Management)
  • Troubleshooting other employees broken reports and documents
