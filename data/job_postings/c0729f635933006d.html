WHAT YOU'LL DO

BCG recently launched the Silicon Valley Office and Digital Immersion Center (SVD), which brings together the complete spectrum of DigitalBCG competencies. One of the key priorities for SVD is to foster BCG’s connection with the Silicon Valley ecosystem. BCG is seeing a strong demand from global clients to get connected to the broader Silicon Valley ecosystem as part of their innovation and digital agenda. To serve this growing need, BCG launched the BCG NxTConnect program to help clients engage with the innovation ecosystem, and support SVD’s overall engagement with the Silicon Valley community. BCG signed exclusive partnerships with firms such as Silicon Foundry, Silicon Valley Bank and several top venture capitals firms in Silicon Valley to leverage their deep expertise and networks in the innovation ecosystem.

The BCG NxTConnect program is aimed at connecting our clients to cutting-edge startups, leading companies and the innovation ecosystem through our partnerships, and complements our other digital offerings including DigitalBCG, B Capital and BCG Digital Ventures.

BCG is looking for a Senior Manager to lead the BCG NxTConnect program and scale it to serve the growing demand from our clients globally. The manager will act as a key bridge between our global clients and the Silicon Valley ecosystem. The manager will lead all activities related to the BCG NxTConnect program in close collaboration with leaders in the Silicon Valley Office & Digital Center. The manager will be responsible for engaging with BCG teams, clients and partner firms in Silicon Valley. As a key part of the role, the manager will make deep connections with players across the Silicon Valley ecosystem, and leverage the networks to drive successful outcomes for SVD, BCG NxtConnect and global clients.

YOU'RE GOOD AT

The responsibilities of the Program Manager will focus on three main dimensions:

Manage BCG NxTConnect program
  • Coordinate programs to connect global clients to Silicon Valley innovation ecosystem of startups, technology companies, venture capital firms, incubators, and influencers
  • Manage requests from BCG teams seeking to engage with the program, and communicate with the teams to provide program details and engagement process
  • Evaluate requests from BCG teams to validate if they meet the defined qualification criteria to engage with the program
  • Act as an evangelist for the program, share program details and successes across various BCG forums and events. Create relevant content (e.g., publications, newsletter, videos, etc.) to share updates on program
  • Set up and manage dashboards and KPIs to track the performance and impact of programs
  • Collect feedback, act as the voice of the customer and improve program offerings
  • Manage internal operations such as weekly meetings, request trackers and dashboards
Support client engagements and innovation events
  • Work closely with BCG client teams to plan and execute client engagements with Silicon Valley innovation ecosystem
  • Facilitate introductions and manage engagement between working teams at our Silicon Valley partner firms (e.g., Silicon Valley Bank, Silicon Foundry, partner VC firms, etc.) and BCG client teams
  • Coordinate with engagement teams to curate the right agenda for client engagements, and help shortlist the right ecosystem players (e.g., startups, etc.) and internal BCG experts to participate in the engagement
  • Manage logistics of client engagements including coordinating visits to the Silicon Valley office or ecosystem players in Silicon Valley
  • Assemble and develop content to support the client engagements and showcase program highlights and BCG capabilities in Digital and innovation ecosystem
  • Host portions of client visits, especially short primers and quick tours/visits
  • Help orchestrate BCG events on latest technology topics along with our Silicon Valley partner firms, craft agendas, manage invite lists and coordinate end-to-end experiences
Manage relationships with Silicon Valley partner firms
  • Liaison with Silicon Valley partner firms (e.g., Silicon Valley Bank, Silicon Foundry, partner VC firms, etc.) to manage partnership and facilitate client engagements
  • Work closely with partner firms to introduce BCG client teams to initiate engagements, curate engagement options and agenda, and provide support throughout client engagement
  • Manage weekly meetings with partner firms to align on key priorities and progress on ongoing client engagements
  • Provide feedback to partner firms to help them improve their program and offerings, and better meet BCG and client needs
  • Help build relationships with Silicon Valley ecosystem, startups and VCs to expand visibility and footprint in the technology startup community
