Intern - Data Analysis Yield Enhancement - Manassas, VA

Req. ID: 140146

Recruiter: Aubrey Tuttle

Are you passionate about data? Micron is looking for motivated students for hands on experience in analyzing semiconductor manufacturing data from numerous data sources to improve yield and quality. You will determine factors that positively and negatively impact yield and quality to help a High-Volume Manufacturing Fabrication facility achieve its goals.


Your responsibilities will include, but are not limited to, the following:

  • Effectively and efficiently monitor and analyze trends of semiconductor manufacturing data across multiple parts by finding the root cause as quickly as possible applying solid data analysis techniques
  • Contribute towards improving the defense line by finding the correlations to inline and tool signals through innovative data analytics approach
  • Use decision making methodologies and problem-solving skills to make decisions affecting Product Yield and quality
  • Escalate risk and concerns to the Fab
  • Develop and deliver progress reports, proposals, requirements documentation, and presentations
  • Update engineers and managers on effects on product yield through appropriate meetings and forums and Action items
  • Contributing expert for subject area knowledge to resolve issues & solve highly complex problems by documenting potentially innovative solutions of new data analysis techniques
  • Learn and expand knowledge of device and fab processing, part operation and testing; become familiar with Probe bins, Probe flow, and fail mechanisms and how they correspond to Fab processing; become familiar with parametric tests and test structure layout

Qualifications

  • Pursuing a bachelor’s Degree in any engineering field, Data Science or Computer Science
  • Understanding of basic statistics and Machine Learning algorithms such as Regression, Random Forest, etc
  • Solid understanding of various data types (structured/unstructured/semi-structured) and ability to perform analytics on those data sources to solve complex business problem
  • Some knowledges on statistical programming languages such as R/Python is preferred
  • Self-motivated and enthusiastic to find creative solutions to the challenges
  • Passionate about learning semiconductor memory and fabrication
  • Drive to succeed and ability to energize the team
  • Highly motivated with good analytical and problem-solving skills
  • Excellent written, communication and presentation skills
  • Possess a continuous improvement process (CIP) mindset where you are always looking for ways to improve the operations of the area
  • Strong work ethics and capability to work independently and in teams
