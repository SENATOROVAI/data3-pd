Vice President, Data Strategy & Analytics - Playa Vista, CA

We are looking for a motivated and exceptionally bright Vice President of Data Strategy and Analytics to join the Fullscreen team. This individual will be responsible for all data needs across both talent and brand businesses. The ideal candidate will deploy both vision and strategy for how reporting, innovation, and efficiencies are connected to the larger direction of data strategy & analysis will be deployed. The ideal candidate would have a passion for and a deep knowledge of both platform analytics, data strategy, and product and engineering connection points. This person will oversee operational oversight of day-to-day data team (analysts and reporting coordinators) along with execution of daily workflow. The candidate will work closely with Fullscreen product initiatives and connect frequently with division leads to ensure all data needs are pushing towards operational efficiencies and further revenue generation. The candidate would also have a proven track record of personal and/or professional growth to support and fulfill the strategic objectives of a highly cross-functional organization.

  • Hold relationships at both Platform and 3rd party vendors to company at the highest level
  • Partner with department heads/subject matter experts to help answer critical strategic questions
  • Lead and manage data teams deploying all internal processes; through monitoring, measuring, and providing feedback on process performance
  • Work across all functional areas to oversee daily data operations across Fullscreen to ensure business goals are being met, and areas of operational efficiency are identified
  • Improve the data operational systems, processes and policies in support of the organization's mission and vision (when applicable)
  • Manage data process change at the senior leadership level; includes assisting with developing training materials and facilitating training sessions
  • Meet with department heads regularly to report on gaps in process, product strategy and project findings as relating to new platform developments and analytics features
  • Develop operational metrics and report to management quarterly on key initiatives and process enhancements
  • Develop and execute on training and workflow process documentation
  • Liaison with senior product members as it pertains to the development and construction of the Fullscreen data projects
