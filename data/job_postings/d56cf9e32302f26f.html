This Position The VP, Global Data is a new role at DocuSign and will be responsible for establishing a vision and an enterprise wide Data Strategy to drive business value and customer success. The Global Data team includes a core centralized direct team and an extensive virtual team across engineering and the business. This person will report to the Chief Technology and Operations Officer Responsibilities
  • Set enterprise wide data architecture ensuring security, functionality, and integration with all core data sources
  • Be the 'Product Owner' responsible for developing, prioritizing and implementing a data roadmap using agile practices
  • Develop and operationalize enterprise wide data policies, standards and procedures
  • Manage, lead and motivate cross-functional, interdisciplinary teams to achieve strategic and tactical business outcomes
  • Drive data security program to ensure DocuSign uses best in class security protection techniques in compliance with required legal and regulatory policy
  • Improve data quality through defined data governance, data management and focused data quality remediation programs
  • Drive performance analytics programs to help DocuSign improve and accelerate decision making through effective use of data, targets, and measures
  • Partner closely with the senior/executive leadership to optimize delivery of data insights, and realization of greater business value
  • e the 'Business Owner' of all data tools, applications, and integration solutions
  • Empower business teams to grow their analytics capabilities as their needs expand, supported by a centralized data expertise and a flexible data platform
  • Keep pace with the changing business environment, technology and data innovation in order to optimize data value and mitigate risks
  • Promote and oversee strategic relationships with external entities including vendors and partners
