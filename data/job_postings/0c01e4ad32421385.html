Production Analyst - Camarillo, CA

The Raspberry Production Analyst is the essential liaison between Driscoll’s and their contracted Substrate Raspberry Growers. This position is tasked with communicating and resolving any issues the Substrate Raspberry Growers might have, while continuously aligning the mutual goals of both parties.

Responsibilities

Preferred Qualifications

  • Bilingual (Spanish and English) is required
  • BS degree in agriculture or other horticultural science is required
  • Minimum of 3 years of experience in the field is expected
  • Knowledge of Raspberry and Substrate production is preferred
  • PCA license is a plus
  • Strong organizational and writing skills is critical
  • Good math and analytical skills are critical
  • High proficiency with Excel spreadsheets and database systems including the ability to create and work with detailed planning models is expected (power pivot and power query knowledge is good to have)
  • Data modeling experience is required
  • Capacity to be fair, impartial, and tactful is essential
  • Ability to work and communicate independently and contribute to group processes is critical
  • Moderate walking and bending during field evaluation processes and ability to lift 50 lbs. is to be expected
  • Extended hours and flexibility with work schedules to accommodate travel, forecasting and field visits is expected
  • California driver’s license and the ability to be covered under company-sponsored vehicle insurance program
  • A valid passport and the ability to travel internationally without restrictions

Leadership Competencies

  • Strategic Mindset: Looks toward the future and designs a way to get there
  • Plans & Aligns: Establishes priorities and desired outcomes, and creates plans to achieve them
  • Drives Results: Pursues everything with energy, resourcefulness, and the need to achieve desired outcomes
  • Collaborates: Works with a variety of colleagues, inside and/or outside of Driscoll’s, seeking win-wins solutions
  • Drives Engagement: Creates a motivating work environment and empowers others
  • Develops Talent: Actively seeks opportunities to develop one’s self and others
  • Courage: Tackles difficult issues timely with optimism and transparency

Candidate Profile

The Raspberry Production Analyst will work with growers to solve production issues, monitor and collect information on acreage, cultural practices, forecasting, varieties, and yields. This position requires the ability to collaborate with the Manager to design and execute field trials in grower’s field in order to provide growers with updates on recommended production practices.

Qualified candidates must be able to analyze data, analyze cost and compile production information to support with strategic planning process.

Lastly, the Raspberry Production specialist must be able to independently communicate Driscoll’s goals and objectives to the Substrate Raspberry Grower base.
