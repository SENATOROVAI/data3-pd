Data Scientist I - Fort Worth, TX 76102

The Data Scientist I is responsible for
  • Implementing the design, development, deployment, and maintenance of predictive/prescriptive/statistical models
  • Modeling with expertise in forecasting, optimization, data mining, analysis, and analyzing complex datasets
  • Conducting studies with the use of descriptive and supervised machine learning methods and advanced statistical methods using innovative and the latest advanced technique and algorithms
  • Summarizing, reporting, and providing polished presentations of findings to a variety of internal clients as well as working with other departments to achieve the overall company objectives
  • Lead in the production of research and analysis to quantify the impact of internal and external environments on portfolio performance. The Data Scientist I is the subject matter expert with an in depth knowledge of quantitative methods and diligent knowledge of data sources and tools
