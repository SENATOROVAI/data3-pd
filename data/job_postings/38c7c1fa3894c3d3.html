Function & Responsibility


Directs and coordinates activities of production department(s) in processing materials and products in machining, assembly and welding by performing the following duties personally or through subordinate team leads.


Primary Responsibilities:


  • Manages direct labor team of welders, machinists and assemblers for optimum safety, productivity and quality
  • Responsible for operator performance including performance assessment, selection & hiring, training and disciplinary action
  • Accountable, along with peers, for On Time Delivery performance and responding to customer escalations
  • Plans production operations, establishing priorities and sequences for manufacturing products
  • Prepares operational schedules and coordinates manufacturing activities to ensure production and quality of products meets specifications
  • Reviews production and operating performance data to identify, prioritize and progress continuous improvement activities
  • Participate in the recommendation, selection and implementation of capital improvement projects
  • Inspects machines and equipment to ensure specific operational performance and optimum utilization
  • Develops or revise standard operational and working practices and observes workers to ensure compliance
  • Develop & maintain a cross-training matrix for the production department
  • Compiles, stores, and retrieves production data
  • Performs other duties as assigned

  • Ability to interpret an extensive variety of technical instructions in mathematical or diagram form and deal with several abstract and concrete variables
