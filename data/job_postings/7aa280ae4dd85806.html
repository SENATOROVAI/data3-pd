Job Description

Inside this Business Group

Non-Volatile Solutions Memory Group: The Non-Volatile Memory Solutions Group is a worldwide organization that delivers NAND flash memory products for use in Solid State Drives (SSDs), portable memory storage devices, digital camera memory cards, and other devices. The group is responsible for NVM technology design and development, complete Solid State Drive (SSD) system hardware and firmware development, as well as wafer and SSD manufacturing

In this role you will be part of a team that is working to enable a data rich environment that supports ingestion, transformation, storage, and analysis of information about the operation of solid state drives. This opportunity includes working with data scientists to apply data architecture and management principles toward enabling an environment which can accelerate business growth and mature the organization's understanding of how to develop new technologies.

This is an opportunity for a computer scientist to gain experience in the realm of data science and be exposed to a tool chain that enables advanced data analytics!. Responsibilities may be quite diverse of a nonexempt technical nature. U.S. experience and education requirements will vary significantly depending on the unique needs of the job. Job assignment open to both full and part time candidates.
