The position supports the Environmental Shared Services (ESS) group with environmental compliance related tasks such as invoicing, store / divisional notifications, submittals, data entry, and reporting in order to meet deliverables and state and federal regulatory requirements.

  • Maintains environmental databases and systems through data collection, scanning, data entry, and reporting
  • Coordinates ESS invoice processing and payments. Works with vendors to address any billing concerns or statement discrepancies
  • Supports the ESS group, other functional departments, and the divisions by answering questions and furnishing information based on company policies and procedures
  • Compiles and communicates routine / ad hoc environmental work to the appropriate teams and individuals
  • Assists in the monitoring, tracking, and processing of environmental agreements, signatures, and reports
  • Develops and maintains positive working relationships with supervisory personnel, co-workers government representatives, and other business professionals
  • Other assigned duties as assigned or deemed necessary

QUALIFICATIONS

  • High school diploma; Associates degree preferred
  • Requires 1 - 3 years administrative/secretarial, environmental compliance management or related experience
  • Must be computer literate in Word, Excel, Access and PowerPoint
