For this Data Analyst role:

What You'll Do

  • Work with cross-functional teams to provide analytical insights and guide strategic decisions to business stakeholders
  • Communicate as a bridge between business needs and engineering, providing analytical support and metrics
  • Build reports, models and visualizations focused on business development and client performance to improve sales and existing relationships
  • Be responsible for development and maintenance of Looker, utilizing the underlying SQL database

Your Skills

  • Analytical Thinking – examine and assess data from a variety of sources, approach problem statements with scientific rigor
  • Business Context – strong understanding of the product and business; quick to identify problem statements, use business context to understand data, and provide perceptive insights
  • Technical Aptitude – use SQL and other engineering tools to empower reporting capabilities
  • Mathematics – excellent quantitative skills to derive insights from multiple data sources and utilize them to develop statistically rigorous models
  • Prioritization – able to juggle multiple analytical projects while being flexible to re-prioritize as needed
  • Collaboration – work closely with Engineering, Product, Sales, and Client Services
  • Communication – strong written and verbal skills to deliver findings to a wide range of audience as well as documenting with precision

Requirements

  • Demonstrated success in a full-time analyst role (3+ years experience preferred)
  • Advanced SQL, Excel and Statistics skills
  • Prior experience working with sales enablement and client services
  • Basic Python or other programming language experience

Bonus Points

  • Prior ticketing/music industry experience a big plus
  • Prior development experience with Looker (or other BI/Data Visualization tools like Tableau, ZOMO, Periscope, Snowflake)
  • Prior work experience with Google Analytics, Mixpanel or other product & user behavioral analytics tools
