Job Description:

Software developer who will be a member of a team responsible for full life-cycle software development; design, implementation, test, documentation, deployment, and sustainment of applications in fulfillment of sponsored requirements

-

Job Details:

Responsibilities

  • Implement software solutions
  • Perform unit, regression, and integration testing as appropriate
  • Create/maintain application documentation
  • Provide technical assistance to users
  • Document and address defect/deficiency reports
  • Work as part of a team to provide detailed software design solutions
  • Other related functions as assigned

Required Qualifications

Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Electrical Engineering, Computer Engineering or other applied sciences. Demonstrated academic or professional experience in software development. Academic or professional experience with at least two of the following: Java, Python, C#, or C/C++. Experience using software configuration management processes. Thorough understanding of object-oriented programming concepts and data structures. Applicant must have a dynamic skill set, willing to work with new technologies, be highly organized and capable of planning and coordinating multiple tasks and managing their time. The position will require attention to detail, effective problem solving skills and excellent judgment. Ability to work independently with sensitive and confidential information, maintain a professional demeanor, work as a team member without daily supervision and effectively communicate. Able to accept supervision. Regular and punctual attendance.

US Citizen. Applicant selected will be subject to a government security investigation and must meet eligibility requirements for access to classified information at the level appropriate to the project requirements of the position.

Preferred Qualifications

Master's degree in area listed above. Experience in oracle-based application development, SQL scripting, Visual Studio IDE. Experience with EF, WPF, MVVM, ASP.Net, JavaScript. Experience with Numpy, Scipy, and GDAL. Two or more years of professional experience as a software developer. Cumulative GPA of 3.0.
