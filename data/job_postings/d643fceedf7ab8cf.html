As a Senior Analyst in Trust and Safety, you will play a critical role working with a cross-functional team to keep our marketplace safe and build a platform for growth.

Your Responsibilities:

  • Identify trends, distill insights, and create models and rules to manage risk and detect fraud, account takeover, and scam attempts
  • Analyze behavior patterns through analyses of large data sets, enhance detection rules, and improve automation with
  • Define and track metrics to support our operations agents in keeping our platform safe for users
  • Work closely with multi-functional teams to translate requirements into technical specifications
  • Partner with program managers to shape the policies of Upwork marketplace and drive continuous improvement to the platform
  • Solve a wide range of business problems creatively and ensure the safety of the Upwork community

What it takes to catch our eye:

  • 2+ years of work experience in an analytics role
  • Strong SQL, Excel, presentation skills and a deep understanding of statistics and predictive analytics
  • True curiosity and a desire to fully dissect a challenge, as well as a strong motivation to protect our customers and our company

How to really knock our socks off:

  • Ability to distilling data into key insights that tell a story
  • Ability to work independently in a small team and a dynamic environment
  • Experience handling large scale structured/unstructured data
  • Familiarity with either Python or R programming
  • Demonstrated real passion for marketplaces
  • Strong interest or previous experience in working in trust, risk, or security
