Database Specialist/Compliance Monitor - San Francisco, CA 94103

SUMMARY OF DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:

Under the supervision of the Director of Housing Services, the Housing Data Specialist / Compliance Monitor provides database system administration, daily data entry, relevant staff training, and coordinates contract compliance activities within the Housing Department.

PRIMARY DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:


1. Perform contract compliance activities including reviewing electronic and physical resident files and staff contract compliance monitoring.

2. Train Housing staff in paperwork completion and database use.

3. Assist in the production of the department training manual.


3. Perform data entry, verification, and data analysis for all housing sites under the Housing Department.


4. Perform system administration duties for the Salesforce database including password retrieval, staff assistance, page customization, and report creation. Act as primary ECS liaison to Saleforce and Exponent Partners regarding system maintenance and updates to the database.

5. Track data entry/paperwork submissions from housing sites, including responding to ongoing data collection issues.


6. Provide weekly and monthly reports for internal supervision, as well as produce client demographic reports and other statistics as needed.

7. Provide monthly reports to the San Francisco Homeless Management Information System (SF-HMIS).

8. Provide support for data collection and periodic reporting requested for quarterly and annual projects for all housing sites, the ECS Housing Director, Support Services Managers, Case Managers and other ECS Managers as needed.


9. Create, maintain, and update filing systems for data entry paperwork and administrative documents.


10. Create letters, documents, forms and memos as requested; typing drafts and final corrections.


11. Fax and copy documents as needed.


12. Assist with special projects.


13. Attend all meetings as assigned.


14. Perform all duties as assigned.
