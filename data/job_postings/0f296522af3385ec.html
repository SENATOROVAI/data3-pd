About the Company
Lincoln Financial Group, a Fortune 250 company with over 10,000 employees, provides advice and solutions that help empower Americans to take charge of their financial lives with confidence. Our core business areas — Life Insurance, Annuities, Retirement Plan Services and Group Protection — focus on supporting, preserving and enhancing over 17 million customer’s lifestyles and retirement outcomes.

The Role

As the Recruiter in our Advisor Based Sales team, you will support the recruitment of advisors from a variety of sources through networking and prospecting. You will also support the onboarding of new advisors.

Responsibilities

Relationship Management
  • Builds and maintains strong business relationships with key sales clients to meet or exceed established sales goals, quotas, targets or objectives for assigned area(s)
  • Serves as a resource to internal/external stakeholders
  • Supports Regional Directors to develop business plans designed to increase financial representative recruitment

Prospecting
  • Utilizes provided departmental script during inbound calls and outbound calls to initiate call campaigns
  • Plans and executes active call campaigns within assigned territory
  • Identifies and qualifies financial representative prospect through call campaigns and using networking platforms such as linked in while maintaining high activity standards that meet or exceed monthly lead generation quotas
  • Create and network with Centers of Influence to identify prospective advisor candidates,
  • Maintains pipeline with national prospective financial representative leads through proactive prospecting and inbound calls
  • Evaluates basic prospective financial representative information as part of screening process
  • Distributes leads to appropriate Regional Directors & collaborates with internal stakeholders to ensure the proper transition of prospective financial representatives
  • Captures recruiting activity (i.e. call logs, call detail and action plan) in contact management system
  • Builds and maintains strong business relationships with key recruiting targets to meet or exceed established goals, quotas or objectives

Business Acumen
  • Maintains knowledge on current and emerging developments/trends, assesses the impact, and collaborates with management to incorporate new trends and developments in current and future solutions
  • Develops and maintains a strong understanding of LFG's products, services and operational structure to enhance ability to identify and target sales growth opportunities
  • Develops and maintains a strong understanding of retail distribution platform to enhance ability to identify and target growth opportunities
  • Develops business on behalf of the Lincoln Financial Group (LFG) in a manner that supports a positive company image, in compliance with all required rules and regulations and ethical in all considerations
  • Remains current in profession and industry trends
  • Makes a positive contribution as demonstrated by: - Making suggestions for improvement - Learning new skills, procedures and processes
  • Identifies and recommends to management process improvements that reduce workloads or improve quality
  • Collaborates with internal stakeholders to ensure the proper processing of all sold products from time of sale to issuance
