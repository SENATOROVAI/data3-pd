Senior Manager/Director, Data Protection Alliances


Job Summary

Thank you for your interest in Nutanix! We are a constantly learning, continuously improving company with immense respect for the law of small improvements. After our successful IPO in late 2016, we are still in hyper-growth mode, with plans of growing almost 3X over the next few years - it's an exciting time to be at Nutanix. Our mission is to help our customers revolutionize their datacenter infrastructure with our Enterprise Cloud OS. We think big, but start small. We believe a well-designed innovation engine is ambitious yet iterative, strategic yet detail-oriented.

We are looking for a Senior Manager/Director of Strategic Alliances to drive our alliances with major software companies in the backup and data protection domain. This person will work with senior executives and cross-functional leaders to recruit and manage our relationships with these partners.

The team’s mission is to translate product strategy into an executional go-to-market model. The ideal candidate will have a proven track record of defining the partner strategy with a bias towards execution and measuring business results. Additionally, you will be responsible for defining and evangelizing the joint value proposition within both the partner organization and at Nutanix.

Responsibilities
  • Develop an overall vision, strategy, and execution plan for Nutanix as we form alliances with the targeted ecosystem of players
  • Identify key areas of alignment focused on specific industries or target growth markets and solutions offerings
  • Develop a partnership roadmap to drive joint innovation and products through the ecosystem
  • Lead negotiation of technology & business agreements with the partner
  • Build and execute the right strategic alignment between Nutanix and the alliance partners, including driving go-to-market programs, solution development, field enablement and technical alignment
  • Evangelize Nutanix to key stakeholders at all levels of the partner organization and represent the voice of the partner within Nutanix
  • Act as a thought leader to Nutanix internal teams on business and strategic issues relating to the Nutanix technology partner ecosystem
  • Establish partner scorecard and track key success metrics; Accountable for Quarterly Business Reviews with partners and internal executives

  • Prior experience working with one of the major data protection providers
  • Recent success in bootstrapping a key go-to-market relationship with demonstrable results
  • A structured thinker with superior writing, presentation, and problem solving skills
  • Expertise in developing and executing GTM strategy including joint value proposition, marketing programs, etc
  • Self-starter with a bias for action; Hands-on, and “does what it takes” attitude to drive success
  • Ability to manage multiple projects, determine project urgency and execute detailed action plans
  • Working knowledge of major industry trends in enterprise software and infrastructure including, but not limited to, Cloud, Enterprise Software, Converged Infrastructure, Software Defined Data Centers, Big Data, etc
  • Exceptional interpersonal skills and able to communicate professionally with partners, customers, and internal cross-functional teams including Product Management, Sales, Marketing, and Legal
  • Able to build strong relationships at all levels (working level to executive level)
  • Proven ability to generate revenue through alliance partnerships
  • Highest level of personal and professional ethics and integrity
  • Mature and confident with the executive presence to effectively represent Nutanix
  • A high degree of passion, energy, drive and willingness to travel (domestic and international)

Background

  • 10+ years’ work experience in tech specifically in Enterprise software, virtualization, and/or Cloud spaces
  • Start-up experience, VC, Corporate Development or other similar business-building experience within a larger company that demonstrates an entrepreneurial mindset
  • MBA or equivalent experience is desired. Bachelors in Engineering or related field or equivalent technical experience preferred
