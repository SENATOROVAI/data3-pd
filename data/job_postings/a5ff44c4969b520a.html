The Boston R&D team is building solutions for travel reservations and we need a talented Intern to help us build it! Our product manages millions of Travel bookings and expenses, including variety of content like flights, hotels, rental cars, insurance, taxi etc.

The internship will occur in the scope of an innovative project where we need to extract meaningful information from fare rules using NLP and all the past data that we have.

What will you be doing?

Do you find the idea of working in a diverse multicultural environment exciting? You will be integrated into a world class engineering team, working with several locations around the world like Nice in France, Frankfurt in Germany, Bangalore in India and interacting with a diverse group of like-minded engineers.

You will design and code a prototype to extract information from the air/hotel fare rules and create structured information from un-structured data.

As a Intern you will help engineers to design the prototype on 2 layers:

  • An AI/ML model to extract the information using NLP
  • A service that takes fare rules, uses this model and returns relevant information

Once we a working model we need to optimize it to achieve the desired levels of accuracy.
