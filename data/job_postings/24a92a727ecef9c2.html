Data Consultant - Oakland, CA

    As a Data Consultant, your role will be to develop, write packages and libraries, deploy models, troubleshoot, and provide technical support for developing predictive models. You will be working with various machine learning and data analysis techniques such as clustering (DBSCANS, TDA etc), forecasting (ARIMA, LSTM) and Auto-ml.

    Other Qualifications
    Strong problem solving skills with an emphasis on product development.
    Experience using statistical computer languages (R, Python, SQL, etc.) to manipulate data and draw insights from large data sets.
    Experience working with and creating data architectures.
    Knowledge of a variety of machine learning techniques (clustering, decision tree learning, artificial neural networks, etc.) and their real-world advantages/drawbacks.
    Knowledge of advanced statistical techniques and concepts (regression, properties of distributions, statistical tests and proper usage, etc.) and experience with applications.
    Excellent written and verbal communication skills for coordinating across teams.
    National Provider Data Management (NPDM) is a shared service established to support regional and national provider data management, reporting, analytics, system strategy, and compliance. NPDM's focus is on improving provider data quality and developing a common business process and technological approach for addressing critical data needs.
    The NPDM Data Consultant provides support in making strategic data-related decisions by analyzing, manipulating, tracking, internally managing and reporting data. This position functions both as consultant and as a high-level SAS programmer. The position works with a diverse set of clients, data owners and stakeholders to ensure business requirements for provider data, whether they pertain to meeting an external requirement or fulfilling an internal business need, are accounted for and translated into technical output in the form of provider network reports, network adequacy analyses, directory extracts or other purposes as needed.

    Essential Responsibilities:

  • Builds and maintains complex SQL and SAS routines using macros, vendor software, and software written by self and others
  • Tests and maintains data integrity across multiple databases (e.g. internal SAS, DB2, VSAM; external: Adobe, Excel, SAS parsing routines, COBOL and other types of databases)
  • Defines and frames complex multi-dimensional issues and develops time tables/processes for decision making
  • Translates business needs, issues, and ideas into effective strategies and action plans and technical output
  • Formulates specific implementation plans and evaluates the effectiveness of actions/programs implemented
  • Develops creative alternative solutions
  • Project manages resource allocation and on time delivery of output
  • Works closely with IT and internal and external partners
  • May coordinate team/project activities and schedules
  • May assist in the determination of goals and priorities with team, clients, or project management sponsors
  • May provide leadership to multi-disciplinary project teams
