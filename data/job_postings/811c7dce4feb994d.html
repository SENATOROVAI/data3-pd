Job Description

Description

SAIC is seeking a Metrics and Data Team Manager to join our MSI team supporting the Commonwealth of Virginia (COV).


  • The Metrics and Data Team Manager provides expertise on continuous process improvement strategies, change management projects, and performance measures


  • The Metrics and Data Team Manager will analyze existing IT service delivery business processes to determine current states, define desired future states, and perform gap analyzes


  • This work includes planning and implementing process improvements via change management and transition planning, and measuring service performance


  • The work may include performing cost/benefit analyzes


  • Service Management processes will be designed to meet or exceed standards contained in ISO/IEC 20000


  • Quality Management processes will be designed to meet or exceed standards contained in ISO 9001. In addition, ITIL best practices for IT Service Management will be applied


  • The Metrics and Data Team Manager will work closely with our Senior IT Systems Integration Analyst to determine functional requirements associated with the concurrent implementation of new technologies and business processes


The Metrics and Data Team Manager will:


  • Solve Complex Problems. Develops technical solutions to complex problems which require the regular use of ingenuity and creativity


  • Have Freedom to Act. Work is performed without appreciable direction. Exercises considerable latitude in determining technical objectives of assignment. Exercises judgment in selecting methods, techniques and evaluation criteria for obtaining results


  • Impact Team Success. Be a team player who contributes directly to the accomplishment of program objectives and goals


  • Be a Liaison. Engage in frequent inter- organizational contacts. Represent the program by providing solutions to difficult technical issues associated with specific projects. The Metrics and Data Team Manager will receive assignments in the form of objectives and establishes goals to meet objectives. Work is reviewed and measured based on meeting objectives and schedules
