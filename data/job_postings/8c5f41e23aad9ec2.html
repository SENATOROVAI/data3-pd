GDIT is seeking an Epidemiologist to perform the following tasks and responsibilities:

  • Develops, conducts, and evaluates epidemiologic data and studies in support of public health
  • Oversees the analysis of data and ensures timely dissemination of the results of these studies
  • Duties may include development and review of study protocols, critical literature reviews, direct participation in data collection, and interpretation of study results
  • Creates, manages, and assesses databases critical to the mission as well as provides internal/external customer service support
  • Utilizes these databases to design studies, answer inquiries, and produce reports
  • Expertise using major medical information systems such as MSS Datamart or MHS Data Repository (MDR) Composite Health Care System (CHCS) or AHLTA
  • Able to perform analytical and inferential statistics to include risk ratios, odds ratios, ANOVA, T-test, chi 2, correlation, non-parametric tests, regression and logistic regression, and survival analysis
  • Fluency with computerized database management and statistical software programs, particularly SAS coding and database management
  • Identifies critical outcome measures, both morbidity and mortality based, for the evaluation of the effectiveness of public health programs
  • Compares outcome objectives for disease prevention and health promotion programs which will enable comparison with other applicable benchmarks
Education
  • Bachelor's degree required in epidemiology, biostatistics, statistics, or public health
  • Master's degree preferred
Qualifications

2 + years of related experience in scientific research and analysis.
