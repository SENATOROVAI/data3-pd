Culture and Engagement Manager - San Francisco, CA 94105

At Autodesk we are incredibly intentional about our culture. The Autodesk Culture and Engagement team is on a mission to embed our recently launched Culture Code in the day to day lives of our 10,000+ global employees. We think deeply about culture and draw upon psychology, learning and development, data science and communications to understand employee experience and build culture engagements that are both transformative and sustainable. As a critical member of the Culture and Engagement Team, you will help drive culture strategy, build programs and execute on global initiatives. This is an individual contributor role in Autodesk People and Places organization, reporting directly to the Global Head of Culture and Engagement.

Autodesk’s employee engagement scores are already best in class. Come join our dynamic Culture and Engagement team to be part of our culture transformation and continue to make Autodesk one of the best places to work in the world.

Responsibilities
  • Work with the Global Head of Culture and Engagement to develop strategies to successfully scale our culture code
  • Analyze culture data collected through our employee engagement survey and other culture relevant feedback, and develop interventions aimed at addressing culture “hot spots.”
  • Facilitate culture related off-sites to further shift leader mindset and behavior
  • Program manage culture initiatives to ensure that our culture transformation maintains momentum
  • Engage in planning for a variety of internal and external events to bring visibility and learning to our employees, our partners and our customers
  • Partner and collaborate with our learning and development team partners to create learning paths directly tied to living our Culture Code
  • Partner and collaborate with our Culture and Diversity & Inclusion communications lead to build relevant culture communications

Minimum Qualifications
  • Five years or equivalent of relevant work experience in culture, employee engagement, or organizational and learning development
  • Experience managing large global programs with strong organizational and written skills
  • Excellent team and large group facilitation skills
  • Ability to analyze quantitative and qualitative data, and make summary recommendations
  • Strategic and creative thinking to help shape culture strategy
  • Experience in diversity and inclusion a plus
