Stress Analyst II - Fort Worth, TX 76177

POSITION SUMMARY: Responsible for stress analyses and substantiation reports for aircraft alterations. Methods include classical hand analysis and finite element modeling. Maintains appropriate documentation. Relies on instructions and established guidelines to perform the functions of the job. Works under minimal supervision. Have knowledge of, and follows engineering and applicable Quality Operating Procedures. Supports the quality management system and quality objectives.

ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS AND RESPONSIBILITIES:
  • The analyst must be able to perform the functions below with minimal supervision
  • Supports design engineers in developing, documenting, creating, and improving new products and more efficient processes
  • Evaluates opportunities to improve performance, increase yields, and minimize product cost and weight
  • Identifies and documents capabilities and limitations of processes
  • Evaluates manufacturing and installation discrepancies and assists in determining effective solutions
  • Performs finite element modeling on selected components in order to accurately determine external and internal loads
  • Performs static stress analyses on components PATRAN or FEMAP software to determine external and internal loads
  • Maintains appropriate documentation for process, unit, and company reports
  • Assumes responsibility for establishing and maintaining effective communication, coordination, and working relationships with area staff, clients, and management
  • Assumes responsibility for related duties as required or assigned

REQUIRED QUALIFICATIONS:
  • Bachelor of Science degree in Engineering or related field
  • 5+ years of related experience

PREFERRED QUALIFICATIONS:
  • Previous aircraft stress experience helpful
