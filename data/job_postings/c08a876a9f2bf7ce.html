Data Analyst, Customer Support - Boston, MA 02210

Overview

As a member of the Care Analytics team, the Data Analyst, Customer Support is responsible for measuring and reporting on the work of our Customer Support teams. Working with many different support teams and processes, this person will be critical to centralizing insights. The preferred candidate will demonstrate the abilty to build custom data sets, identify correlation between different data points, and visualize them for the Customer Support organization.

Responsibilities

  • Work with the Customer Support teams to unify reporting, identify areas trends, and advise on process improvements
  • Combine structured and unstructured data from various sources to report on the business at large
  • Analyze Support teams’ performance to help leaders identify areas of opportunity and improvement to drive better customer experiences
  • Work with Digital Support team to build reporting of holistic Customer Support experience

Qualifications

  • 3+ years of related experience
  • Experience working with relational databases (HIVE, Oracle, SQL Server, etc.)
  • Bachelor’s degree in business or analytical field preferred
  • Moderate/Strong SQL development skills: writing complex queries, transforming data, and mining structured and unstructured data
  • Beginner/Intermediate Python skills desired: Pandas, Matplotlib, NumPy, Tensorflow
  • Experience with Tableau or other data visualization tools preferred
  • Knowledge of Customer Support and Call Center metrics preferred
  • Good written and verbal communication skills
