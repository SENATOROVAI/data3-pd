NTT DATA Services currently seeks a Intelligence Analyst to join our team in Merrifield, Virginia (US-VA), United States (US).


ANALYTICAL TOOLS: Utilize tools to track money laundering and structuring activities of transnational criminal organizations. Evaluate and/or create techniques, activities, and tools for criminal, money laundering and counterterrorism case analysis. These tools will be available on Government Furnished Equipment (GFE).

LINK DISCOVERY: Assess organizations for networks and linkages. An example of this task includes analyzing what other U.S. agencies, have regarding information, and determine, based on heuristic models, the actionable value added of this information. When appropriate apply social network analysis.

NOVEL TRADECRAFT: Evaluate and combine tools such as enhanced open source, commercial data, and government data sources to assist in development of lead-based information and identification of assets for possible seizure. These tools shall be utilized to further exploit data.

INNOVATIVE ANALYSIS: Perform information development and dissemination; specifically by enabling operators to develop better insights based on the above tools and tradecraft. Also update training, processes, and mentoring approaches based on lessons learned in this task. In addition produce, manage content, and disseminate threat reports, to include liaison activities, intelligence analysis, evaluation, and case integration. Provide predictive analysis to field agents and analysts.

Specific Work May Include:

Respond to incoming field requests to support ongoing federal, criminal investigations.

Conduct analysis, using the OFC’s combined law enforcement and financial data, to produce actionable leads for field agents working against international organized crime.

Provide intelligence products that details criminal activity and assets available for seizure, for prosecutors to target the criminal proceeds of international criminal organizations.
