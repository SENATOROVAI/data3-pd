Modeling and Simulation Engineer - Level 2 - Seattle, WA

Boeing Global Services has an opportunity for an Early Career Modeling and Simulation_Design Engineer in Seattle, WA.

Position Responsibilities:

  • The following tasks are split between Modeling and Simulation Engineers on the DS&A simulation team
  • Maintain Alliance core and coordinate with other stakeholders
  • Develop new capabilities in Alliance Core and Airline Sim
  • Build LSAS models including customized constructs in the ExtendSim environment
  • Use simulation capabilities to analyze, assess and identify root cause factors, perform trade studies and sensitivity analyses, and provide recommendations to Boeing program management
  • Develop visualization tools for new and existing models

    Required Skills/Qualifications:

  • Bachelor’s, Master’s, or Doctorate or Science degree from an accredited course of study, in engineering, computer science, mathematics, physics, or chemistry

  • Modeling and Simulation
    Strong knowledge of programming languages (e.g., C++). General knowledge of modeling and prototyping tools (e.g., ExtendSIm, Matlab). General knowledge of computing equipment and its operating systems (e.g., Windows, Linux). Knowledge of software development and testing tools (e.g., editors, compilers, linkers, desktop simulations, configuration management tools, requirements management tools) capability and usage
  • Analytical Skills
    Skill and ability to: collect, organize, synthesize, and analyze data; summarize findings; develop conclusions and recommendations from appropriate data sources at the work group level
  • Process Modeling
    Apply general knowledge of modeling techniques (e.g., mathematical modeling, simulation, design of experiments, operations research) to analyze a system
  • Statistical Concepts
    General knowledge of statistical analysis concepts such as normal distribution, standard deviations, mean, probability, and confidence levels.

    Desired Skills/Qualifications:

  • Operational Effectiveness Analysis
    General knowledge of operational effectiveness analysis methodologies (e.g., mission and system effectiveness analysis) and tools (e.g., mission simulations; measure of effectiveness; human in the loop simulators, test facilities and operational evaluations)
  • Advanced Math & System Model
    General applied knowledge of mathematical modeling or advanced mathematics to provide information for known problems, under close technical supervision
  • Analysis/Design Optimization
  • General ability to evaluate complex vehicles and systems from a multitude of perspectives. Includes the ability to apply figures of merit and integrated optimization tools. Ability to perform multi-disciplinary trade studies to evaluate configuration alternatives for functional and spatial integration

Other Desired:

  • Simulation experience with emphases on event-based simulation. Specific simulation experience using ExtendSim, Simeo, AnyLogic or equivalent software package. Experience developing in R, Python, Java Script, NodeJS and Visual Studio. Data visualization using Tableau and/or other visualizations tools. Domain expertise in supply chain, logistics and fleet sustainment, military systems analysis, reliability analysis, optimization techniques
