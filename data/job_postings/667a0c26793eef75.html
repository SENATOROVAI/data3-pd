Basic Qualifications:

To be considered for this position, you must minimally meet the knowledge, skills, and abilities listed below:

  • Bachelor's degree in Business Administration, Accounting, Finance, or related discipline with 8 years of industry related experience in finance, accounting, or program control
  • Three+ years of leadership experience directly managing people
  • Strong experience with Earned Value Management
  • Understanding of DOD contracts, government Cost Accounting Standards, and the FAR
  • Prior experience with government program budgeting and forecasting, as well as Annual Operating Plan development
  • Proficient in Microsoft Office suite, with summary and reporting knowledge of SAP, and COGNOS
  • Prior experience preparing and presenting financial analysis to team and/or management
  • Ability to obtain a US Secret level clearance. US Citizenship is a prerequisite

Preferred Qualifications:

Candidates with these desired skills will be given preferential consideration:

  • MBA
  • Experience with COGNOS and Cobra
  • Experience with financial forecasting systems
  • Current US Secret level clearance.
