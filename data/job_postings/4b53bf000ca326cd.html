Basic Function

This position reports to the North America Olefins “commercial” organization, to develop, designs, and implement collaborative data analytics and visualization solutions for interactive assessments to solve business related problems. The solutions encompass the economics, pricing, optimization of profitability models, market dynamics, manufacturing processes, and other commercial requirements directly related to maximizing the profitability of the business; thru data visualization, analytics, and models.

This position reports to the Manager of Business Solutions & Data Analytics, Olefins North America and is located in Downtown Houston at our North American headquarters and offers a competitive base salary, bonus, 401K matching, generous benefits package, pension plan and employee discounts.


Roles & Responsibilities
  • Business Solutions & Application Development - Support implementation of new analytical tools and techniques to support business decisions related to: model strategy, data analytics, data regressions, visualization, scheduling, and integration of new concepts to improve profitability, forecasting, optimization, planning, & business drivers
  • Tableau – Collaborate with commercial team to develop, expand, and maintain Tableau based dashboards and visualizations for better business decisions
  • Data Analysis using SQL - Use Relational databases to perform data analysis and build visualizations
  • Data Source Quality & Prep – Implement data quality/prep techniques to ensure organization’s data integration and ingestion for Optimization, Visualization, and Analytical software applications are sustainable and high quality
  • Software/Hardware – Learn and Support implementing the commercial related activities related to new software & hardware requirements for the organization processing & analytical capabilities. Related to: Tableau, R Server, Cloud based storage, Metadata Management, PIMS, Spyro, Aspen IP21/Process Explorer, Aspen Advisor, etc
  • Training & Maintenance – Serve to advance, train, and influence the organization to become users of new models, methods, tools, and analytics. Implement new applications, coordinate with analysts, and maintain documentation and governance of the Olefins business data analytics processes
