Data & Applied Scientist - Redmond, WA

Do you want your work to positively impact the lives of millions of people? Are you up for the challenge of working with terabytes of data at scale?

The Modern Life Machine Learning team is looking for a Machine Learning Scientist who can develop and optimize bleeding edge machine learning models partnering with a highly multi-disciplinary team of data scientists, strategic partners, product managers, and subject domain experts. Our team of entrepreneurial applied machine learning scientists works across a broad portfolio of Microsoft consumer products, representing a $400B market opportunity, to develop innovative ML models.

Responsibilities

In this role you will have the opportunity to:
  • Solve challenging problems at significant scale
  • Work in a start-up like environment with a strong, supportive corporate culture
  • Research and implement appropriate ML algorithms and tools according to requirements
  • Adapt standard and custom machine learning methods to best exploit modern parallel environments
  • Extend existing ML libraries and frameworks
  • Perform statistical analysis and fine-tune using test results
