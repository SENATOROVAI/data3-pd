Aerodynamics Airplane Performance Engineering Entry Level - Seal Beach, CA 90740

Boeing Commercial Airplanes Flight Operations Engineering is seeking an entry level Aerodynamics Engineer - Airplane Performance to join our team in Seal Beach, California. As a Flight Operation Engineer you will Standardized Computerized Aircraft Performance (SCAP) and Enroute software and databases, develop, code and test enhancements to post-processing tools. Come be a part of Flight Operations team and be a part creating and help publishing the Boeing Flight Operations Manuals.

This position is located in Seal Beach, CA and offers relocation based on candidate eligibility.

This is a Non-Union represented position

Primary Responsibilities:

  • Perform takeoff, landing, and enroute performance analysis in PC and UNIX environments utilizing Boeing
  • Standardized Computerized Aircraft Performance (SCAP) and Enroute software and databases
  • Post-process performance data to meet document data coverage and format requirements by utilizing custom Fortran, MSExcel, Visual Basic, Matlab and Perl-script based post-processing tools
  • Develop, code, and test enhancements to post-processing tools
  • Incorporate performance data directly in the FCOM and FPPM publication systems, which are Adobe Framemaker and XML based
  • Assemble and test Boeing Enroute databases
  • Coordinate requirements for database and other data deliverables
  • Assess proposed regulations and changes to aircraft certification that could impact aircraft performance and aircraft performance documents or databases
  • Review new performance document releases and approve for publication
  • Review organizational process and workflow regularly for continuous improvement
  • Assist in resolution of questions pertaining to aircraft performance data, databases and documents
  • Develop aircraft performance data to be published in the Boeing Flight Crew Operations Manual (FCOM), Flight Planning and Performance Manual (FPPM), and iPad Quick Reference Handbook application (IQRH)
