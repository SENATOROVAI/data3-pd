The Boeing Company is seeking a Senior Quality Systems Specialist to support the BCA Customer Support organization. This position will provide Quality Management System (QMS) support to Customer Support and its functions; Maintenance Engineering, Service Engineering, Flight Operations Engineering, Operations Center, and Fleet Chief Offices. This position will conduct annual AS9100 QMS reviews, manage the Corrective Action record, execute formal Records and Information Management audits and monitoring, provide document maintenance (BPIs, LWIs, BPGs, etc.), and maintain a Customer Support QMS Forum for integration of Quality across the businesses. This position is located in Seal Beach, CA.
