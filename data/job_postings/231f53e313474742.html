Process & Automation Engineer - Menlo Park, CA

The revolution in enzyme engineering will be built on top of datasets of enzyme reactivity unparalleled in both size and quality. To power world-class machine learning models, we will be pushing the boundaries of laboratory automation and leading the way towards faster, more robust and accurate robotic sample processing systems to acquire data at levels of throughput orders of magnitude above anything that has been previously built.

Additionally, we will bring a fundamental paradigm shift to ultra high-throughput screening by introducing clinical-diagnostics-levels of attention to data quality as of yet unseen in the industry to guarantee the data acquired is representative of the true, underlying activity of enzymes.

As an early member of the automation and process engineering team, you will lead the charge to develop complex automated systems and elegantly scale Aether’s ability to acquire high quality data. You will be responsible for the design and development of the entire set of technologies that will make up a state-of-the-art, next-generation enzyme design and evolution factory. You will help shape Aether Biomachines’ long term vision for versatile automated solutions to support the indexing of enzyme function and stability across multiple dimensions, such as amino acid sequence, cofactors, post translational modifications, reaction buffers, etc. You will be in charge of finding technical answers to process problems and interface heavily with the other technology groups: machine learning, protein sciences, software engineering and mass spectrometry. You will use your strong background in mechatronics (software, electrical engineering, robotics, data analysis, etc.) to lead the development of novel, advanced systems supporting enzymatic sciences.

You will help grow a fast-paced, high-output band of engineers, hackers, and builders, and play a critical role in shaping the technical environment in which the automation group executes.

You will work daily with industry and thought leaders in laboratory automation, enzyme sciences, chemical manufacturing, etc. Through hard work and dedication, you will power the creation of the most advanced robotic systems supporting a revolution in chemical manufacturing.
