STATISTICS AND RESERVES APPLICATION DEPARTMENT OVERVIEW
The Statistics and Reserves application suite (STAR) collects information from Banks and other types of Depository Institutions. STAR analyzes and processes this collected data, providing valuable and critical information to the Federal Reserve Board of Governors, Federal Financial Institutions Examination Council (FFIEC), Federal Economists, the Treasury, and Congress. STAR also calculates Reserve Balance requirements, monitors maintained Reserve Balances, and delivers accounting statements to Banks and other Depository Institutions.
