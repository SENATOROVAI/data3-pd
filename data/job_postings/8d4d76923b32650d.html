Data Analytics Manager, Royalties and Accounting - New York, NY 10011

We are looking for a Data Analytics Manager to work with our Royalties and Accounting data team. Your mission will be to support the data operations for royalty calculations, perform analytics over royalties and product related data, and manage a team to provide insights to internal and external stakeholders.

What You’ll Do

  • Manage a team of data analysts / scientists working with large data sets to solve complex problems in a dynamic environment
  • Manage the prioritization and delivery of configuration changes, testing and deployment for new product offerings and contractual requirements in our royalties and reporting systems
  • Manage the technical components of our monthly payable generation process ensuring the timely creation of payables, completion of quality checks, and analysis of payable outputs
  • Work with our engineers and data scientists to support the development of system and process enhancements to support royalties calculations, product configuration and reporting
  • Work with accounting team members to define requirements for ad-hoc data analysis and reporting, being able to work independently to propose solutions to address business needs
  • Support ad-hoc requests from various internal teams (Accounting, Legal, Licensing, Financial Planning, etc) as well as external music licensors that require analysis of royalties and streaming data
  • Work with a group of highly motivated individuals who are passionate about Spotify’s success

Who You Are

  • You have a minimum 5 years of experience using databases and analytic tools to solve business problems
  • You have experience managing and developing a team of data analysts / scientists
  • You have a degree in mathematics, statistics, computer science or another quantitative discipline
  • You have excellent verbal, written and interpersonal skills and the ability to prioritize and deliver results in a fast moving company with a wide variety of strategic initiatives
  • You have experience working with financial data including performing data reconciliations and quality assurance testing
  • The ideal candidate has previous experience working with configuring and testing royalties systems, and/or performing analysis royalty related data

Desired Skills

  • SQL
  • Python / Pandas
  • Big Query / Google Cloud Platform
  • Tableau, Qliksense, or other visualization tools
  • Command Line Operations
