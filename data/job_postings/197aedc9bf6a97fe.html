Director of Data, AI@Unity - San Francisco, CA 94107

We are looking for an exceptional leader as Director of Data for Unity. The Unity Engine is a premier platform for a variety of different interactive media experiences, ranging from console and phone games to Hollywood film CGI, cutting-edge AR/VR interactive experiences and industrial simulations. AI@Unity brings the power of data and Machine Learning to every corner of Unity and its ecosystem. Through our evolving set of platforms and services, we enable Unity teams and our external customers to learn about and transform user data into insights, decisions, and products. We work with the best and brightest in AI and distributed systems to bring impactful changes to the areas of Games, Autonomous Vehicles and Robotics. The central Data organization is part of AI@Unity and is integral to Unity's evidence-driven decision-making. Billions of gamers and millions of developers use Unity and produce data that we ingest and transform into insights for our business. We are a comprehensive Data organization comprising of the Data Platform (catering to storage, compute, and transport for events on the scale of 10s of billions a day), the Data Engineering team (which builds services to help all of Unity derive intelligence from this data), and the Data Science team (which does predictive modeling and experimentation to drive product improvements). We help everyone find high quality, well-documented data by building the right data sets, evolving them to meet changing business needs, and evangelizing their application to business problems of all kinds. We are focused on enabling teams at Unity to be more effective, not necessarily answering data questions directly. As Director of Data, you will lead the entire Data organization, across engineering and product management. This is a highly visible leadership role with cross-company impact. Your ability to provide vision, develop strategy and ensure strong execution for this core function of Unity is key to your success. You are deeply knowledgeable about Big Data technologies (for example Kafka, Spark, Beam), and have built platforms at scale with these. You care deeply about observability and performance monitoring and have built, launched and operated enterprise-level production platforms that have stringent SLAs, clear documentation and quick on-boarding experience. You have worked on analytics applications, experimentation research, and experiment design, and understand what's critical for your business stakeholders in terms of new features and algorithms, data visualization and other tools to open up insights to the whole company, improving every area of the business. You will pursue these challenges for Unity as a thought leader and key executive partner. You will lead and further grow a world-class team, you will debate and influence business and technical strategies to further entrench Unity as the world's premier RT3D platform, and be a key contributor in our mission to enable creators world over.
