UBA Team

Splunk User Behavior Analytics (UBA) is a machine learning driven solution that helps organizations find hidden threats and anomalous behavior across users, devices, and applications. You will be part of Splunk UBA team and work with other team members, engineering and Product Management to plan, develop and deliver various features and functionalities for the Splunk UBA.

Role

Splunk’s Enterprise Product area is looking for a seasoned engineering leader to help develop and grow a team of passionate engineers to develop and improve our core Enterprise product in San Jose, CA.

You are someone who thrives in a dynamic environment and has a track record of technical expertise and management responsibility. You will be responsible for driving your team to work closely across multiple engineering areas and locations. As we drive forward for the success of our customers you will hold the team accountable to achieve the goals of engineering excellence and product value.

What You'll Do

  • Excellent leadership and communication skills. Ability to effectively communicate complex concepts to executives and business sponsors in a clear and concise manner

  • Champion an atmosphere of continuous improvement by serving as a coach, mentor, and technical advisor


Knowledge, Skills and Abilities
