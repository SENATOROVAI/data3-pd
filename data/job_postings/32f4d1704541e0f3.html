Position Summary:
NHL Seattle is seeking a Data Engineer who will manage the data used by decision-makers across the Hockey Operations organization. The Data Engineer will be charged with designing and supporting the acquisition, movement, storage, and utilization of data across the team. Additionally, the Data Engineer will be responsible for collaborating with Quantitative Analysts, Developers, and other Hockey Operations staff to effectively utilize the data in analysis and decision making.
Essential Duties & Responsibilities:
  • Design and maintain database and information systems for the Hockey Operations department
  • Develop and manage processes to integrate datasets from various sources and vendors into our data warehouse
  • Build data quality assurance tools to ensure data integrity
  • Work with rest of Research & Development staff to optimize querying and retrieval of information
  • Assist with the display and visualization of data and its integration into Hockey Operations applications
