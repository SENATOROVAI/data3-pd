Lonza in Portsmouth, NH is looking for an experienced Data Scientist to join our Manufacturing Science and Technology (MSAT) team. As our Data Scientist, you will be responsible for developing real time process monitoring tools and analytics used for fault detection as well as prediction. This individual will be required to execute the efficient exploration and interpretation of complex data-sets by constructing statistical models to summarize and explain process variability and equipment anomalies. He or she will interface daily with Process Experts to mine, clean and explore data sets used to identify causal factors as well as potential opportunities to predict ideal process outcomes, optimize timing of critical events, process productivity, product quality, and general process knowledge. The role will be required to identify and apply appropriate modeling or statistical techniques suitable to unique problem statements.

Key responsibilities:

  • Participate in the investigation of both basic and complex process events and anomaly investigation
  • Develop automated predictive process monitoring tools and systems
  • Data analysis via “standard” methods, e.g. Regression Analysis, Clustering Analysis, Dimensionality Reduction Analysis
  • Perform Data mining, cleaning and data-set prep in order to execute complex multivariate statistical analysis
  • Develop and apply AI and Machine Learning (ML) to applications of data mining, prescriptive analytics, process monitoring
  • Works with operations technologies teams to support data collection, virtualization and integration

Key requirements:

  • Masters of Science degree in Biological, Data Sciences, and Chemical Engineering area preferred
  • Demonstrated understanding of related industrial or biopharmaceutical manufacturing processes, data analysis via “standard” methods (e.g. Regression Analysis, Clustering Analysis, Dimensionality Reduction Analysis) with ML/AI expertise
  • Experience in manipulating / managing complex data-sets, Oracle and SQL database querying/manipulations, Multi-Variant Analysis testing and optimization, Extract Transform Load tools
  • Natural sciences background a plus
  • Excellent written and verbal communications skills to document relevant findings and investigational process in clear structured manner are required
