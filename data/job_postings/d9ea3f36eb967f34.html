Sr.Quality Engineer - Irving, TX

We are seeking a talented individual for Senior Quality Engineer who will serve as a change agent focused on end to end quality assurance and Lean/Six Sigma initiatives across all product/service lines and Company deliverables. The Quality Engineer will create robust defect remediation plans that are effective and sustainable, along with implementing appropriate controls and measures (SPC, KPIs, etc.) to help process owners maintain improvements. The Senior Quality Engineer will be responsible for working to improve organizational capabilities including facilitating company growth, customer loyalty, cost reduction, improved efficiency, and employee engagement through the application of high-impact quality and business optimization initiatives. This position will assist in transforming enterprise culture by solidifying the corporate Quality and Six Sigma organization as a data-driven, fact-based, and objective arm of the Company as we drive toward operational excellence. Provides statistical information for quality improvement by identifying testing methods and samples.

Essential Responsibilities:
  • Analyze and suggest improvements to business processes to improve overall workflow efficiency and effectiveness, with a focus on delivering results
  • Apply process improvement and re-engineering methodologies utilizing Lean and Six Sigma sciences to identify and capitalize on opportunities for improvement
  • Perform non-conformance investigation, root cause analysis, and corrective/preventative action efforts throughout the value stream
  • Develop, implement, and conduct analysis on meaningful reports to facilitate decision making
  • Drive accountability throughout the organization to an end state of zero defects and maximum efficiency
  • Contribute to the design and implementation of enterprise-wide understanding, communication, education and coordination of all programs related to Quality, Lean, and Six Sigma
  • Work closely with internal stakeholders to establish and maintain effective relationships, resolve issues, and facilitate positive organizational change
  • Provide quality, lean, and six sigma coaching, and mentoring to team members and internal stakeholders
  • Determine quality improvement parameters by identifying statistical methods relevant to manufacturing processes
  • Establish statistical confidence by identifying sample size and acceptable error; determining levels of confidence
  • Establish statistical reliability by quality tools and methodologies
  • Develop sampling plans by applying attribute, variable, and sequential sampling methods
  • Maintain statistical process controls
  • Analyze data by completing hypothesis, normal distribution, and process capability analysis tests
  • Prepare reports by collecting, analyzing, and summarizing data; making recommendations
Non-Essential Responsibilities:
  • Assist in the creation of key performance metrics and scorecards
  • Implement / improve remediation initiatives across functions
  • Document processes and procedures using standard methodologies and tools
  • Gain expertise in quality and business optimization by staying abreast on best practices and methodologies
  • Maintains professional and technical knowledge by attending educational workshops; reviewing professional publications; establishing personal networks; benchmarking state-of-the-art practices; participating in professional societies maintaining certified quality engineer qualification (CQE)
