Consultant - Berkeley, CA

As a consultant, you will typically manage project teams that range from three to fifteen people and will be involved from the start to the finish of the project. You will participate in and help oversee everything required in an engagement, including collection, organization and cleaning of data; evaluation of client issues; data analysis; and communication of results.

Throughout your career, you will receive the highest level of responsibility and benefit from regular interaction with members of the team at all levels.

Responsibilities

  • Develop analytical strategies and work plans in an environment of ambiguity and incomplete information
  • Conduct rigorous subject-matter and academic research and empirical analyses
  • Manage teams, provide feedback and mentorship, monitor and control quality
  • Draft reports and communicate results of analysis to clients in a clear, non-technical manner
  • Contribute to the growth and development of the practice through client engagement, marketing, and training

Qualifications

  • Doctorate degree in economics, statistics or other quantitative sciences is highly preferred
  • Extensive experience with analytic and statistical techniques
  • Advanced knowledge in programming and/or statistical packages such as Stata, Matlab, R, etc
  • Ability to think and act with a focus on the needs of the client
  • Excellent written and spoken communication skills
  • Comfortable working with all levels of staff and clients
  • Ability to juggle many simultaneous tasks and regularly reprioritize to meet client and company goals
