Position overview

Our Omnichannel Strategist work with clients to solve their brand challenges, grow their business, and define and achieve their objectives. You will partner with our Creative and Technology teams to bring these strategies and solutions to life. You are an expert at diving into client’s business, working with multidisciplinary teams, and making the complex simple to present well-defined, fully thought out, and integrated marketing solutions that leverage every channel at your fingertips in the right way , to influence our audience and drive desired outcomes.

Responsibilities:

  • Develop targeted and integrated omnichannel marketing strategies for brands that build profitable, long-term relationships with our clients
  • Successful development and management of strategies that lead and grow our clients’ brands
  • Research and collaborate with our planning department to understand existing digital landscape, target audience, and the channels that influence them along their journey. Distill this information into the most relevant insights to develop actionable market assessments
  • Ensure all digital programs have clearly defined metrics (KPI definition) to evaluate success and continuous, measurable improvement in client campaigns; and collaborate with our analytics department to continuously analyze and report back to our clients on their programs’ successes, and clearly define opportunities
  • Maintain subject matter expertise, and proactively and persuasively deliver timely guidance on digital trends impacting clients’ business
  • Provide account teams with consistent, valuable, and relevant guidance for strategic projects
  • Partner with our technology and in-house experts to craft ecosystems, driver strategies, nurture programs, and integrated plans that successfully drive key objectives
