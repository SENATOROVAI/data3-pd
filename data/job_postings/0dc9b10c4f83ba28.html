Sales Development Intern - San Francisco, CA 94107

Unity Technologies is looking for an intern to join the Sales Development team, which partners with the advertising sales team via pre-sales and post-sales support. The sales development team helps our brand sales team bring in non-gaming clients across consumer industries (entertainment, automotive, etc.), with a mixture of mobile video and custom interactive ad units. We are looking for a detail-oriented, organized, and highly motivated intern. You will be partnering with our creative strategist and producer to help with RFPs (requests for proposal) by joining creative brainstorms, pulling necessary 1st party and 3rd party data, and helping build out PowerPoints and Excel spreadsheets. This exciting position will enable you to work with Unity's sales, marketing, product, ad operations, and legal teams to build your knowledge and understanding of the mobile advertising landscape. Responsibilities
  • Develop a strong understanding of Unity Monetization products
  • Assist with preparation of media plans and proposals
  • Brainstorm creative executions that meet RFP goals, and align with Unity product offerings
  • Build app lists and targeting recommendations using a combination of internal and external resources
  • Assist with the review of insertion orders and help to communicate legal feedback with the sales team
  • Work with producer and creative vendors to vet and price out creative ideas as needed
    • Requirements
  • Excellent knowledge of PowerPoint and Excel
  • Strong verbal and written communication skills
  • Proven ability to manage multiple projects at a time while paying strict attention to detail
  • Comfortable working in a fast-paced environment
    • Bonus points
  • Interest in video games and/or emerging media formats
  • Photoshop / Design experience
