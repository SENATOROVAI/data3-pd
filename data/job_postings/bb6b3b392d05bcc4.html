The Challenge

The Adobe Experience Cloud business unit is looking for a Data Scientist for Adobe Target.

Adobe Target is a leader in the market for rigorous statistical testing, targeting, optimization and content delivery infrastructure. Target empowers the world’s largest brands and marketing organizations to enable personalization at scale. Our mission is to enable marketers to comprehensively optimize on revenue and engagement.

Inside Adobe, we build the personalization engine that powers the experience cloud. There, we form the foundation of a machine learning experimentation, recommendations and optimization delivery engine.

Our system leverages petabytes of data, marrying the data from Adobe Analytics and Adobe Audience Manager with the experience composition and delivery of Adobe Experience Manager, Adobe Campaign and Adobe Media Optimizer solutions.

What you’ll do:

  • Own the data science strategy for Adobe Target. Decide the areas of investment for data science efforts
  • Work with Product Managers and Engineering Leaders to layout the data science roadmap
  • Design the framework that ties together all the statistical model for A/B testing and personalization
  • Manage the lifecycle of models. Select the right model for the problem, develop it managing within constraints, evaluate performance and have monitoring in place

Skills you have:

  • A deep understanding of statistical and predictive modeling concepts, machine-learning approaches, clustering and classification techniques, and recommendation and optimization algorithms
  • Experience of building and evolving various offline and online models
  • Experience with tuning model performance and testing the impact of model changes
  • Ability to work with engineering and evaluate the trade-offs between architectural changes and analytical solution
  • Ability to write Python or R code
  • 2+ years of experience with BS/ MS/PhD

Preferred Skills:

  • Experience of software development on big data and/or cloud based technologies would be a plus
