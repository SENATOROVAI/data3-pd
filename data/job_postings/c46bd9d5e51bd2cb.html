About the Data Analyst Position

As a technology leader, we have high-traffic websites and store massive amounts of data that we need to slice and dice every day. We are looking for a Data Analyst that will be part of a growing, world-class team supporting our account management and site optimization groups. The ideal candidate for the Data Analyst role will be a key resource for analytic best practices within Study.com and for analyzing and querying data in a fast-paced Internet business environment.

The Data Analyst must be passionate about digging into data and understanding the stories the data are telling. You will be equally passionate about helping others solve problems, gain insights, and make decisions using data.

Responsibilities:

  • As part of the Business Intelligence team, own and maintain the entire data warehouse, including tens of millions of events coming through a Kafka queue each day, a 15 TB relational data warehouse, and integration with Google BigQuery
  • Work in a cross-functional team and be the team's point person for any data related tasks (ETLing, analysis, visualizations, etc.)
  • Monitor and analyze the team's A/B tests and proactively communicate key insights within your team and to external stakeholders
  • Be the voice of data by supporting the team's product owner in making data driven decisions and defining KPIs
  • Actively establish processes and build tools to make data more accessible to everyone
  • Use your knowledge of statistics to support the Business Intelligence team in building and maintaining machine learning models (logistic regressions, recurrent neural nets, etc.) that drive a significant portion of our revenue
  • Help and teach others throughout the company to better use and understand our data

Requirements:

  • A passion for learning and a willingness to pick up whatever you need to to get the job done
  • 4+ years' experience in a data analyst role, experience with web analytics is a plus
  • Solid MySQL/SQL experience or other related databases
  • Experience working on ETLs/data pipelines
  • Solid experience in Excel for data analysis and visualization
  • Strong analytical skills and ability to draw conclusions based on data
  • Comfortable working in a fast-paced, dynamic environment while juggling multiple projects
  • BS in Mathematics, Statistics, Computer Science, Operations Research, or similar fields
  • Good interpersonal communication and presentation skills
  • Self-motivated, detail-oriented and passionate

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Reporting and Business Intelligence experience (Tableau, QlikView, Microstrategy, etc)
  • Proficient in using statistical software (R, MATLAB, SAS, etc.)
  • Hands-on experience with advanced statistical concepts, such as cluster analysis, singular value decomposition, stochastic gradient descent, and Bayesian methods
  • Knowledge of web analytics software and best practices
  • Knowledge of some scripting language such as Groovy, Python, Perl, or in a compiled language such as Java or C++
  • Higher degrees such as Masters or PhD
