POSITION PURPOSE

This position will provide increased data analytics capabilities to the Business and Data Intelligence (BDI) team. This role will take operational, financial and market data from varying business units and deliver intelligence and insights to be the foundation for company business decisions and strategy. This role will collaborate with other members of the BDI team, data and information vendors, and other corporate teams as needed to learn requirements and collaborate on projects. In their approach to accomplishing this roles tasks, work will specifically require dedication towards problem solving, divergent thinking, intellectual curiosity, pursuing independently developed ideas, and pattern identification.
