Health Science Specialist - San Francisco, CA 94102

Duties

Summary

This position is located in the Research Service, San Francisco VA Health Care System, VA Sierra Pacific Network VISN 21, Veterans Health Administration of the Department of Veterans Affairs. The incumbent is a Health Science Specialist and is a member of the research team for the Cooperative Studies Program (CSP) study. This position is responsible for the overall conduct of the study under the direction of the Local Site Investigator (LSI) at San Francisco VA Healthcare System.

Responsibilities

Some of the duties and responsibilities of this position include (but are not limited to):
  • Reviews the Computerized Patient Records System (CPRS) to identify potential research subjects;
  • Builds/maintains ongoing relationships with potential referring programs within the VA;
  • Assesses potential enrollment barriers;
  • Designs recruitment strategies and utilizes recruitment tools to maximize patient participation;
  • Designs, develops, and documents quality control systems for data validation;
  • Independently explains study protocol (rational and procedures) to potential study participants;
  • Educates study participants about medication;
  • Obtains informed consent;
  • Ensures that all aspects of the consent process are met, and collect data while following study protocol;
  • Collaborates with LSI to develop policies and procedures governing the local conduct of the study within local, federal, and protocol guidelines;
  • Follows the study participants throughout the study and ensuring completion of study assessments;
  • Ensures study participants are consented appropriately;
  • Manages and maintains applicable study approvals (i.e. CIRB and R&DC);
  • Enters study data to database;
  • Sorts and analyzes study data
Performs other related duties as assigned
