Description

  • Perform research, protocol analysis, logical and physical mapping of data focusing primarily on the network through the application layers of the OSI model, and report the results of the analysis

  • Utilize experience with collection assets, with emphasis on access pursuit and recommending and implementing future collection operations
  • Perform queries and analysis using agency tools and databases
  • Perform Signals Analysis Research and Target Development

- Perform Social Network Analysis - Draft formal and informal reports of your findings - Brief a variety of national and tactical consumers, orally and in writing - Perform fusion of multiple intelligence disciplines
