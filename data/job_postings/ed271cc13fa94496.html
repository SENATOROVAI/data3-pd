Senior Data Scientist - United States

Our data analytics team for healthcare Fraud Detection is looking for additional experienced Senior Data Scientist to serve as a technical resource in the design and development of new predictive modeling initiatives. It involves predictive modeling, data mining, deep learning, SQL, Hadoop, Spark etc. This is a flexible opportunity. Work remotely or work part time is OK.

Qualification Requirements

  • Graduate degree (M.S. required, Ph.D. preferred) in a quantitative discipline
  • 2+ years professional experience building predictive and descriptive models
  • Experience, and expertise in diverse statistical and data mining techniques (e.g. - GLM/Regression, Boosting, Random Forest, Trees, Clustering, PCA, SVM, text mining, NLP, social network analysis etc.)
  • Demonstrated proficiency with one or two statistical packages such as Spark ML-Lib, Python, R, or SAS
  • Ability to program in either of Python, Spark, Scala, R, or SAS is highly desirable, Spark Prefrred
  • Experience with neural network deep learning is desirable
  • Understanding of RDBMs and SQL programming skills are a must
  • Experience with Big Data technologies like Spark, Kafka, Mesos, Hive, NoSQL, etc., and Cloud technologies (AWS, Azure, etc.) is desirable
  • Aptitude for picking up new technologies is expected
  • Experience with healthcare fraud detection with claims or EMR/EHR a big plus

Responsibilities

  • Participate in data cleansing, variable creation, variable transformation, etc., as well as best-practices in the creation of analytic datasets
  • Serve as the technical lead in model development and validation analyses - from driving pragmatic practice of methods to devising novel solutions and diagnostic measures, to coaching and mentoring junior staff
  • Develop process and metrics to monitor model performance
  • Provide input to help Product Management on implementation specifications and production testing
  • Review reports and make recommendations for needed model enhancements
