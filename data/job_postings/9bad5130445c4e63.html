Responsibilities

As Product Management Personalization Director you will lead cutting-edge, algorithm-centered innovation projects aimed at delivering new experiences for Nike members while driving business value to Nike. In this highly visible and high-profile role, you will help define and execute the product vision and strategy, lead the end-to-end lifecycle of a product, and help identify market opportunities. You will define and execute thorough roadmaps and product priorities based on a strong understanding of consumer needs and data science. The PM Personalization Director will influence, educate, and advocate on tactical product decisions as well as the strategic decisions to enable the next slate of amazing experiences for Nike members.

  • Minimum of a Bachelor’s degree. Post graduate education (MS or PhD) in an applied quantitative field or equivalent experience at a cutting-edge company preferred
  • Demonstrated experience using mathematical modeling and machine learning to solve business problems
  • Demonstrated experience as a product manager, ideally with a consumer technology product
  • Familiar with traditional PM methodologies (e.g., Agile, Kanban, etc.)
  • Record of strong product vision and execution
  • Ability to thrive in a fast-paced, flexible environment
  • Organizational leadership and influence without relying on hierarchy
  • Outstanding written and verbal communication skills
  • Deep understanding of statistical hypothesis testing and A/B methods
  • Passionate about machine learning and personalization
