As we grow, your career and opportunities will grow as well. ---------------- Position Summary ---------------- The Project Manager, Process Engineering is responsible for evaluation of existing business processes for the purpose of identifying and executing on improvement initiatives. This individual will be developing key projects to improve quality, reduce cost, increase productivity and improve cycle time, resulting in significant business improvement and customer satisfaction. The Project Manager will be a subject matter expert on the end-to-end customer journey and work closely with Sales, Partner Operations, Customer Onboarding, Customer Support and Product teams. ------------------------ Primary Responsibilities ------------------------
  • Collect and document business requirements for project and process improvement/automation efforts
  • Drive root cause analysis to solve complex business problems, develop and implement solutions in support of cross-functional partners and business stakeholders
  • Develop subject matter expertise for multi-faceted problems and deliver simplified solutions, eliminate wasteful activities
  • Foster relationships with key cross-functional partners and internal stakeholders; establish strong partnerships to obtain desired results
  • Develop and maintain end user documentation as well as provide training related to business process or system changes;
  • Review information and trends to ensure that the output of processes are achieving the desired results and that services are meeting agreed upon service levels
