Support Data Analyst - United States

Grasp Technologies, Inc.
Job Description


Job Title: Support Data Analyst
Reports To: Director, Client Services
FLSA Status: Exempt

Summary: Responsible for using your broad knowledge of technology and business
processes to think of creative ways to solve problems and uses your impact assessment
and communication skills to appropriately escalate and implement change by performing
the following duties.

Duties and Responsibilities include the following. Other duties may be assigned.

1. Assesses all client support requests effectively and prioritizes accordingly. *

2. Ensures all basic or “first-tier” support questions are being properly handled
within the appropriate time frame. *

3. Grows first level supporter’s skills and abilities consistently to be able to handle
the client the first time without escalation. *

4. Performs “second-tier” support by researching and resolving more complex
issues and must be able to discern when something should be escalated to
development or management. *

5. Identifies system modification opportunities to reduce end-user problems,
streamlines applications, and helps make the customer, both internal and
external, more self-sufficient. *

6. Communicates system and process improvement opportunities to internal Grasp
teams, provides training, infrastructure network services, and application
development, and has the ability to translate the customer’s needs into engineer
speak, aka “the interpreter”. *
