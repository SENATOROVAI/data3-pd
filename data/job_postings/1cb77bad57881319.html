BIOLOGICAL SCIENCE LAB TECHNICIAN - Albany, CA

Duties

Summary

This position is located in ARS, Field Organization, Pacific West Area, Albany CA.

Responsibilities

  • Participates as a team member with the scientist in all phases of the research process and assumes full technical and operational responsibility for specific phases of the research
  • Interprets and documents findings by preparing information to be included in manuscripts or preparing reports summarizing progress or results of research
  • Contributes to the planning of various steps in experiment or project and to interpretation and documentation of findings
  • Retain detailed records of experimental data which involves arranging, analyzing, and summarizing data using personal computers and software packages
