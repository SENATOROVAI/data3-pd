Software Product Manager, Framework and Applications - Deep Learning - Santa Clara, CA 95050

Deep Learning is an exciting new area of machine learning. To bring AI to life, neural networks need to be trained and deployed with domain specific knowledge. Training and inference can be integrated into applications in the data center or at the edge, such as in automobiles, drones, medical devices, and more. Some of the common applications using NVIDIA deep learning software are image classification, object detection, recommendations, conversational virtual assistants, and reinforcement learning.

As a Deep Learning Applications Product Manager you will be working closely with our external customers across all business verticals as well as major cloud providers and internal customers, such as the NVIDIA Drive, Robotics, Research, and Jetson platform customers. You will need to understand the popular applications for deep learning (e.g. Computer Vision, Speech and Audio, Natural Language Processing and Recommendation Engines), the neural networks for those applications and how they can be optimized and deployed to target GPU accelerators.

As Product Manager, you will lead key pieces of NVIDIA's portfolio of software and platforms that are enabling breakthroughs in artificial intelligence through scientific computing and deep learning. You will engage with customers, define products and roadmaps, develop business strategy, devise marketing positioning, and take your products to market. You will have the ability to adapt strategies to changing business environments, along with a strong background in software for technical computing and programming SDKs.

The Product Management organization is a small, strong, and visible group both inside and outside of NVIDIA as our team is focused on deep learning use cases to drive the company's strategy. We need a self-starter to continue to grow this area and work with customers who are on the cutting edge of AI.

Do you have the rare blend of both technical and product skills? Are you passionate about groundbreaking technology? If so, we would love to learn more about you.

  • Define and Prove: You will collect insight and build market analysis that provides the data to enter or change new markets through a clear value proposition for your product

  • Market intelligence: Understand your developers and champion their needs through roadmaps and priority setting as well as competitive solutions

  • Build and Deliver: Collaborate with engineering to set requirements and priorities

  • Product launches: Define early access programs (beta) and go-to-market launch strategies as well as contribute to the cross-functional execution across Marketing, Public Relations, and Sales.
