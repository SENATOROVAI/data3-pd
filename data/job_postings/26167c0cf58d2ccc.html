Contract and Product Operations Associate - Mountain View, CA

Minimum qualifications:

  • Bachelor's degree or equivalent practical experience
  • 2 years of experience in operations, systems management, project management or process improvement
  • Experience with G Suite applications (Gmail, Docs, Sheets, etc.) or similar collaboration software
  • Authorization to work in the United States

Preferred qualifications:

  • 3 years of experience working with contracts and business process systems within a legal department
  • Experience running complex projects with multiple stakeholders
  • Experience using data to identify trends and improve processes or workflows
  • Ability to successfully collaborate cross-functionally with all levels of internal and external stakeholders

About the job

Our Legal team needs more than attorneys to keep things moving. Our Legal Operations team is charged with improving and maintaining the Legal team's internal processes, systems and tools. Responsible for tracking objectives, coordinating financial planning and solving technology challenges, you are entrepreneurial, proactive and highly organized. You keep costs down, engage third-party vendors and make the Legal team's services better, faster and more efficient.

The Contract & Product Operations team provides operational support to our legal contracts and product teams in a variety of ways. The majority of our time is spent on managing contract templates across various systems. We also run projects for many different things that come up in the legal contract or product space. Our mission is to provide top level support to our legal teams and this means going out of our way to understand the issues, provide guidance, help others, work collaboratively, and keep a positive can-do attitude.

20th century laws don't always solve 21st century problems, and Google Legal crafts innovative approaches for handling some of the toughest legal challenges of the information age. Whether you're a patent attorney, an intellectual property expert or an engineer headed to law school, Google Legal lets you take on unanswered legal quandaries and create new precedents. Our innovative services raise challenging questions that demand creative and practical answers. We provide those answers by working at the crossroads of the law and new technology, helping Google build innovative and important products for users around the world.

Responsibilities

  • Serve as a hands on support team (systems expert, contract template expert, project manager, etc.) for the Product and Agreement Legal Teams
  • Collaborate with Legal team members and cross-functional stakeholders (including Systems Engineering teams and others) to prepare contract templates for inclusion in our systems
  • Manage special projects and analysis as required
  • Work cross-functionally to keep various data and sites updated to ensure relevant information is being presented
  • Gather information, analyze and present data to detail how the team spends their time
