As a member of the Finance, Risk and Compliance team, you will have the opportunity to provide quantitative, technical and business consulting our client’s Risk Management functions. What You’ll Bring with You:

  • Strong Business Analyst experience in the Market Risk area
  • Strong knowledge of financial products and its use in market risk management
  • Strong understanding of yield curve construction methodology, bootstrapping techniques and scenario generation processes for VaR calculations
  • Strong analytical and reasoning skills
  • Proven financial and communications skills
  • Ability to perform analysis on large data sets and identify anomalies
  • BA degree in business, finance or related field. Master's degree in economics, finance, statistics or equivalent is a plus
