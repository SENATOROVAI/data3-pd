Sr. Big Data Developer - Washington, DC

Overview

Pyramid Systems is seeking a talented Senior Big Data Developer to join our growing team in Washington, DC.

Responsibilities

  • Develop Big Data solutions using Hadoop tools and supporting technologies for Federal agency
  • Provide expert guidance to agency customers and management teams in related solutions

Qualifications

Required Skills

  • Minimum of ten (10) years of IT experience and at least 3 years working with Hadoop tools and technology including Web Services (RESTful /SOAP) & Web Scrapping (HTTP, CSS and HTML)
  • Proficient with designing and developing big data solutions
  • Experience with widely-used Hadoop tools such as Apache Spark, Hive, Sqoop, Flume and Oozie
  • Extensive experience working with complex file formats include structured, unstructured and semi-structured data including, but not limited to JSON, XML, CSV etc
  • Experience working with cloud based technology such as Amazon EMR, Redshift. Proficient using Python, Java/Java Script including libraries such as Beautiful Soup, urllib, Selenium
  • Experience working with databases and appliances such as Netezza, Oracle, Sql Server, PostgreSQL & Sybase
  • Experience with data modeling
  • A bachelor’s degree in computer science or related field.

Desired Skills

  • Certified Developer from a major hadoop distributer such as Cloudera, HortonWorks and MapR is a major plus. Hadoop developer certifications from other distributers may only be considered at the government’s discretion
  • Strong presentation skills are a major plus.
