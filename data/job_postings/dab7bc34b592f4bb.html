Lockheed Martin is looking for a dedicated Ground Systems Engineer to join the Operations, Sustainment & Logistics (OSL), Satellite Engineering, Operations & Sustainment Support (SEOSS) leadership team primarily focusing on Next Generation Geo (NGG) operations.

You will guide the integration of emerging technologies as we establish the infrastructure that will represent the basis for decades of satellite development and sustainment support. As a thought leader and advisor to the SEOSS manager, you will provide insight into emerging technologies while collaborating across the leadership team to integrate common platforms and products wherever feasible. The SEOSS embedded infrastructure includes a wide variety of technologies in a 24x7 operational environment with high levels of customer interaction. Understanding the effect your recommended technologies will have on embedded legacy technologies is also critical so working across programs (such as OPIR NGG and SBIRS) will be common and expected. Typical responsibilities include:

  • Analyze the technology environment and set forth a collaboration framework to support the corporate digital transformation initiative

  • Interact with stakeholders and evaluate vendor products to ensure the solution fits the goals and vision for modernization and commonality across SEOSS programs

  • Make recommendations to purchase hardware and software; coordinate analysis, engineering, installation and verification activities, and provide backup recovery plans

  • Perform troubleshooting and root cause analysis to resolve complex problems and anomalies

  • Author or drive the development of documentation for Standard Operating Instructions (SOI) / CONOPS where applicable
