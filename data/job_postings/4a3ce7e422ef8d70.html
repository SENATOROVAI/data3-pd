Machine Learning and Computer Vision Engineer (multiple levels) - San Francisco, CA 94102

Who We Are

Neya Systems, the industry leader in developing off-road autonomy for commercial and defense customers, has multiple openings (junior to senior) in our computer vision / machine learning group. What You Will Do

Responsibilities will vary, depending on your experience level, but examples include:

What We Are Looking For

Various experience levels are welcomed. Basic requirements for the position include:

  • Bachelors, Masters or Ph.D. in Robotics, Computer Science, Electrical Engineering, or related discipline
  • Experience with sensor data processing, environment modeling, large-scale point-cloud processing
  • Experience with a subset of machine learning algorithms (e.g. classification, filtering, deep learning, segmentation, etc.)
  • Excellent mathematical reasoning skills, especially with probability
  • Comfortable writing high-quality code in Linux/C++11 or C++14
  • Understanding of sensor error modeling
  • Passion for developing advanced robotics and intelligent systems
