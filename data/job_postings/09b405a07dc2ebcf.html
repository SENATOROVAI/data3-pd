TECHNICAL INFORMATION SPECIALIST (WEB SERVICES) - Monterey, CA

Duties

Summary

You will serve as a Technical information Specialist (WEB SERVICES) in the Dudley Knox Library of NAVAL POSTGRADUATE SCHOOL.


Responsibilities

  • You will analyze organization, layout and content utilizing usability testing, personal development, and other website assessment methodologies
  • You will develop, enhance, and maintain web-based content and services
  • You will utilize analysis tools and processes to analyze transitional statistics in support of library collection development and service improvement efforts
  • You will install, maintain and deploy desktop computers for a variety of users

Travel Required

Occasional travel - You may be expected to travel for this position.
