Position Summary

The Casual will assist with conducting data analyses using statistical software and epidemiologic methods. S/he will also perform literature reviews, present progress reports, and assist with data management. S/he will also assist with applications for data access.

Responsibilities

  • Data analysis: 75%
  • Grant writing, assist in IRB submissions, manuscript preparation and presentation of materials for conferences and lectures: 20%
  • Perform other related duties as assigned: 5%

Minimum Qualifications

  • Requires a bachelor’s degree or equivalent in education and training, plus two years of related experience

Preferred Qualifications

  • Master’s degree in a quantitative field including biostatistics, epidemiology or statistics

Other Requirements

  • The successful candidate must have experience with epidemiologic studies and must be able to conduct analyses using statistical software such as SAS and R
  • He/she must have experience with data analysis, including linear and logistic regression and must be able to independently learn new statistical and epidemiologic methods to apply in observational studies
  • He/she must be detail-oriented, with excellent organizational, interpersonal, written and oral communication skills, and the ability to maintain the highest degree of confidentiality and diplomacy at all times
