ENGINEER/SCIENTIST - Dahlgren, VA

Duties

Summary

You will serve as a ENGINEER or SCIENTIST in the Strike Weapons Systems Division, Weapons Control & Integration Department of NAVSURFWARCENDLD.


Responsibilities

  • You will serve as the System Development Activity (SDA) Test Lead for Tactical Tomahawk Weapons Control System (TTWCS) testing
  • You will coordinate with personnel to assure that all necessary hardware, software and personnel are available to test the TTWCS components
  • You will provide leadership for all test activities ensuring the NSWCDD H50 Test organization provides all products required to satisfy milestone reviews
  • You will assume a significant leadership role for the overall testing of the TTWCS as the SDA Test Lead
  • You will be responsible for working with the Program Office and other organizations in order to coordinate testing of both the TTWCS and other systems that interface with the TTWCS
  • You will provide technical specifications and cost/schedule estimates for emerging test events

Travel Required

Occasional travel - You may be expected to travel for this position.
