What we stand for

We’re looking for a dynamic, strategic leader who provides perspective, inspires confidence and enables breakthrough ability to measure and inform all of the work that marketing does. Marketing ROI is critical to our ability to secure the necessary investment to drive this business and also demonstrate how Marketing is being accountable and effective with the investment from the business.

The Marketing Impact Director will serve as a true voice of measurement and accountability. The person in this role will guide a practice that integrates and builds best in class approaches that demonstrate the impact marketing is having on the business. The portfolio of services include best in class brand measurement along with the opportunity to build models/measurement to demonstrate the impact HP marketing is having across consumer and commercial customers in the short and medium term. The ideal candidate will work across businesses and need to have effective collaboration skills working with finance, category and marketing partners.

A predisposition for collaboration and relationship-building with key stakeholders, team members and external partners is a must. This strategic and forward-looking individual will assess the ongoing needs across businesses, introducing best practices and demonstrating innovative thinking related to modeling, insight and perspective. .
