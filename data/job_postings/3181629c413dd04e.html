Project Cost Controls Analyst-PCA - San Ramon, CA

POSITION SUMMARY:

We are currently seeking Full Time level Project Cost Analyst for natural gas and electric utility projects in San Ramon and Concord.

Read through the RESPONSIBILITIES, QUALIFICATIONS, and DUTIES CAREFULLY to obtain a thorough understanding of the position and skill level we are seeking for this position. No relocation assistance is available for this position.


RESPONSIBILITIES:

The Project Control Analyst position provides support to Project Managers on various Utility projects with collaboration and guidance of the Project Manager/PCA Supervisor. If assigned, may perform functions in the Project Support Function; the Central Data Management Function; and/or the Central Financial Analysis Functions.
