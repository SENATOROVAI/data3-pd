Cyber - Digital Network Intelligence ( DNI ) Analyst - Fort Meade, MD

OVERVIEW

We are seeking a Cyber - Digital Network Intelligence (DNI) Analyst to support our customer in Fort Meade, MD.

RESPONSIBILITIES

  • Conduct Target Development and exploitation of computer networks and / or mobile communication applications
  • Utilize SIGINT databases to develop targets and analyze data
  • Analyze development of new exploitation capabilities against known and newly identified targets
  • Analyze intelligence information from multiple sources for a national intelligence agency

QUALIFICATIONS

REQUIRED:

  • BS degree, with eight (08) years of relevant experience. Equivalent experience may be considered in lieu of degree
  • Shall have training in a technical field, military intelligence training and eight (08) plus years of scientific and technical intelligence analysis experience and knowledge of computer analytical software
  • Shall have eight (08) plus years of Digital Network Intelligence (DNI) analysis experience
PREFERRED:
  • SIGINT Development (SIGDev); SIGINT tools, techniques and processes
  • Analysis of geo locational data and sources
  • Knowledge of wireless technologies such as GSM, CDMA, LTE
  • DNI and/or DNR tools
  • Data normalization
  • Knowledge of telecommunications structures, including circuit and packet switched networks and converged networks
  • Net+, Security+, CEH, CCNA, Convergence+ and/or Geospatial Information Systems (GIS) Certifications are preferred
  • Familiarity with Wireless Network TCP/IP and sub netting
  • Knowledge of IPv6, routing concepts, Net Mapping and Target Templating
  • Social Network Analysis methodologies
  • Writing skills a plus and ability to work with senior reporters for some positions
  • Information assurance and/or cyber skills and terminology is a plus for some positions

SECURITY CLEARANCE:

  • This position requires the candidate to already possess an active TS/SCI with Polygraph clearance and to maintain the clearance.
