PURPOSE OF JOB: The data analyst will be responsible for preparing, analyzing,

and presenting data findings to bring business and customer

insights and to support the objectives of the PriceSmart.com and

Aeropost.com Ecommerce team. This includes extracting and

cleaning the company’s datasets which are currently housed in

PriceSmart’s data warehouse; analyzing data using statistical

tools, regression analysis, and machine learning to identify

causal mechanisms and strategic advantages; and presenting

data in a visualization format to support the E-Commerce team

as well as PriceSmart and Aeropost’s senior decision-makers.

Related specifically to the e-commerce team, the data analyst

will be participate in the design, tracking, and assessment of

strategic merchandise pilots online and will lead the analytic

efforts related to these pilots. Finally, the data analyst will

support the Ecommerce team in maintaining and building

automation tools, utilizing existing and new systems, to help

streamline and bring efficiencies to the organization.

KEY RESPONSIBILITIES: • Extract and manipulate data from PriceSmart’s Domo Data

warehouse for use in E-Commerce strategic initiatives (utilizing

SQL and other database management tools).

• Prepare compelling data analytic and visualization models and

reports to support E-Commerce team leadership and

management.

• Construct econometric and machine learning models showing

causal and correlative impacts and trends in member behavior,

item and category performance. Additionally, include and utilize

macro-level country and regional characteristics when necessary.

• Design strategic item pilots and A/B tests. Utilize analytics tools

to track the associated impacts and present findings in an

actionable and clear manner.

• Prepare data-driven reports and strategic insights for PriceSmart

stakeholders outside of the E-Commerce team

  • Support development of the advanced analytics
practice: Develop best in class advanced analytics practice for
PriceSmart e-commerce including text/search analysis,
predictive modeling, and forecasting.
  • Automation: drive improvements in team processes through
application of technology and partnership with the IT and
Development teams.
  • Data Infrastructure: In collaboration with infrastructure in IT
teams, make recommendations for data architecture strategy.
  • Tool Assessment: Develop and deploy analytical tools and data
science techniques to analyze complex data sets.
  • Monitor trends in data science and alternative sources to ensure
that PriceSmart is in tune with best in class tools and techniques.
