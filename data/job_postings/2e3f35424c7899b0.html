Job Summary : Ensures integrity of data by auditing data; pays invoices; provides information to clients. Follows internal controls according to the company’s Sarbanes-Oxley policy.

Essential Functions:

1. Pays invoices by verifying transaction information; entering data including direct entry, PO matching, work order matching.

2. Accomplishes job results by maintaining relationships with internal and external clients.

3. Ensures integrity of data by researching and reconciling data variances; corrects, deletes or re-enters data.

4. Ensures accuracy of daily entries by auditing source documents.

5. Scans entered invoices to LaserFiche for document retention.

6. Serves customers by providing information and resolving problems.

7. Receives, audits and inputs employee expense reports for reimbursement.

8. Receives and organizes vendor statements; checks vendor files for payment of outstanding invoices listed and resolved any issues related to past due outstanding invoices.

9. Takes phone calls and answers any questions from assigned division or vendors in regards to invoice payments. Contacts vendors to clarify questionable statement items.

10. Performs other related tasks as assigned by the Supervisor. Contributes to team effort by accomplishing related results as needed.

Qualifications

1. Requires High School diploma; Associate’s degree preferred.

2. Requires 1-2 years of Accounts Payables experience in a high volume environment.

3. Proficiency with 10-key calculator required.

Must be computer literate in MS Access, Excel and Word; experience with working with accounting applications preferred.
