The Actuarial Analyst role is responsible for accurately analyzing dental experience. In this role you will conduct the statistical analyses that support forecasting, reserving, fee development and financial reporting. You will also assist customers with creating tools and reporting functions.

How you will make an impact:

  • Project future results based on historical data
  • Develop analysis and review results for reasonableness
  • Advise and provide guidance to internal customers
  • Prepare and present documents to support analysis to internal customers
  • Build and automate tools for customers

What we look for:

  • Bachelor’s degree in Mathematics, Statistics, Actuarial Science or equivalent education is preferred, but candidates with proven experience will not be excluded from consideration
  • 3+ years of experience is preferred
  • SQL and VBA experience
