Description
About Syngenta:

Syngenta is a leading agriculture company helping to improve global food security by enabling millions of farmers to make better use of available resources. Through world class science and innovative crop solutions, our 28,000 people in over 90 countries are working to transform how crops are grown. We are committed to rescuing land from degradation, enhancing biodiversity and revitalizing rural communities.


About this Job:
  • Have you dreamed about having a career in the data science field? Do you want to be part of a team that is solving real world problems such as food security for the world?
  • Join a highly dynamic team that will mentor and cultivate your skills in one of the most interesting career fields in the IT industry. Build the skills in the latest technologies that will spring board your career
  • As a Data Engineer you will utilize your engineering background and desire to learn to work as part of an engineering team to support data scientists in their data pipeline needs

Essential Duties & Responsibilities:


  • Learning and using modern data preparation, integration and metadata management tools and techniques
  • Recommending — or sometimes even automating — existing and future integration flows
  • Collaborate with Sr. Engineers and scientists to define technical deliverables and business outcomes
  • Design and own solutions to maintain high quality standards
  • Proactively communicate plans and updates to colleagues


Qualifications
Education & Experience:
  • 0-2 years’ experience – professional experience/internships are preferred
  • Computer Science/Computer Engineering or comparable degree
  • Demonstrated experience in at least 1 programming language (ex: Python, Java, R, C++, Go, etc.)
  • Portfolio of achievements (projects, hobbies)
  • Strong analytical and problem-solving aptitude
  • Strong communication skills
  • Demonstrated leadership qualities:
    • University leadership/Campus Involvement experience
    • Contribution to open source communities

Additional Knowledge, Skills, Traits & Abilities:

  • AWS experience or knowledge
  • Visualization skills in R Shiny or Python Plotting Libraries (MatplotLib, Pandas, ggplot, Plotly etc.)
  • GIS image and machine learning knowledge
