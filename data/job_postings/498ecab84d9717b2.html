Director, Data Science - Austin, TX 78716

The Director of Data Science oversees daily operations and strategic direction of Data Science team of analysts, data scientists, and engineers. Leads innovative solutions through exploratory data analysis from both structured and unstructured, diverse datasets typically measured in gigabytes or larger. Applies knowledge of statistics, machine learning, programming, data modeling, simulation, and advanced mathematics to lead team in recognizing patterns, identifying opportunities, posing business questions, and making valuable discoveries leading to prototype development and product improvement. Fosters an environment that encourages a flexible, analytical approach to design, develop, and evaluate predictive and prescriptive models and advanced algorithms that lead to optimal value extraction from the data. Understands analytics and statistical software such as SQL, R, Python, Hadoop and others to lead a team who utilizes this technology to perform analysis and interpret data.

Primary Responsibilities:

  • Lead advanced analytics and data science team to support complex analytic projects from ideation to execution, with an emphasis on care delivery, clinical performance, and outcomes
  • Works collaboratively with other areas of IT and key business areas to produce innovative solutions with sophisticated techniques and intuitive visualization that creatively addresses critical issues in healthcare data
  • Build predictive and machine learning models, evaluate performance, and operationalize into a production setting by partnering with data scientists, analysts, engineers, and operational teams
  • Act as trusted SME for numerous advanced analytic methods, strong knowledge of advanced and predictive analytics. Collaborates with end-users to evaluate business goals, identify needs, and propose solutions using the portfolio of technical assets
  • Evaluate performance of existing predictive models, drive ongoing data acquisition from a broad range of sources, and operationalize models into a production setting by partnering with data scientists, analysts, engineers, and operations teams
  • Assist and guide team to research a problem to ascertain the cause in the backend data, uncover the true reasons data patterns and skews may be showing up, and convert those findings into clear data-backed recommendations to influence future strategies
  • Develop presentations around insights and tell compelling stories with the data, and deliver to constituents at all levels: strategic, tactical, operational, and across functions: clinical performance, operations, finance, etc
  • Identify any impact of industry or regulatory changes to improve policy, procedures, and efficiency
  • Technical Expertise in such items as:
    • a. Languages: R, Python, SQL (Oracle, ANSI, Hive, Pig), Scala
    • b. IDEs: Visual Studio, RStudio, Jupyter, Toad, SQL Developer, Rapid SQL
    • c. Frameworks: Hadoop / HDFS, Spark, Oracle, TensorFlow, PyTorch, Keras
    • d. Libraries: Scikit-learn, Pandas, Seaborn, NumPy, SciPy, LightGBM, NLTK
    • e. Platforms: Kafka, Neo4j, SQL Server, MySQL, MS Access, MongoDB
    • f. Environments: Windows, UNIX, Linux
    • g. Visualization: Tableau, MS Power BI, ggplot, matplotlib
    • h. Tools: Provider Profiler, Fraud Data Predictors, SSIS, iWay, MS Office Suite

You’ll be rewarded and recognized for your performance in an environment that will challenge you and give you clear direction on what it takes to succeed in your role as well as provide development for other roles you may be interested in.
