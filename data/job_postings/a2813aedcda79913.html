Job Summary:

This is a unique opportunity to gain broad experience, have significant responsibility for a variety of projects, engage with senior-level decision-makers, and influence strategic business decisions. The Data Analytics and Insights Specialist will be responsible for creating analytics frameworks, analyzing business performance, interpreting business/market data, and providing actionable information to Senior Management and CEO staff.

Reporting Structure:

The Data Analytics and Insights Specialist will report to the Manager of Business Analytics & Insights as a part of the Financial Planning and Analysis team. There are no direct reports reporting into this role.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Understand requirements, gather data from various data sources, conduct business analysis, and provide quantitative insights through presentations, reports, and dashboards
  • Obtain, manipulate, and analyze data to answer specific or open-ended questions, utilizing statistical approaches where applicable, and distill actionable insights
  • Recognize, develop, and adopt best practices in reporting and analysis: automation, data integrity, test design, analysis, validation, and documentation
  • Collaborate with all business units to understand the business challenges and provide the necessary analytical and data support using primarily modeling skills to deliver business outcomes
  • Ability to analyze and interpret a mix of qualitative and quantitative data, construct compelling narratives, and provide recommendations on complex topics
  • Conduct analysis and turn insights into actionable changes for predictive models or policies
  • Mine and translate data into meaningful insights and recommendations for the operational and strategic business units
  • Analyze web traffic and site behavior website's key performance metrics indicators (KPIs) using Google Analytics data. Derive for customer and business insights to support business decisions and drive on improved customer site experience
  • Assist with user acceptance testing and participate in the development of requirements for and implementation of necessary website tagging through Google Tag Manager
  • Manage various internal ad hoc requests and projects to assist and support Senior Management and peers with on-demand analytics
