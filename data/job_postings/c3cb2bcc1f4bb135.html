Position Summary

Life Time is looking for a Sr Data Scientist who will join the Enterprise Business Intelligence team at our corporate headquarters in Chanhassen, MN. The Sr Data Scientist is responsible for providing business partners with data driven analysis/recommendations based on deep analysis of existing and new data sets. The position will also play a key role in the maturity of the Enterprise Data Science practice at Life Time through the introduction of AI/ML best practices and tool sets along with implementing and defining a standard consumption layer for analytical outcomes.

Job Duties and Responsibilities

  • Performs deep analysis on large datasets and provides recommendations based on findings
  • Visualizes data into a consumable format that helps drive business results
  • Determines common algorithms for predicative analytics that can be applied to multiple business pyramids
  • Utilizes Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning tools to enhance predictive analytics capabilities
  • Models data sources, such as IoT, for easy consumption by data tools/analysts
  • Performs data cleansing of disparate systems
  • Trains data models for consumption and accuracy
  • Exposes mined data to data analyst community via repeatable service layer
  • Matures Data Science capabilities at Life Time
  • Mentors Data Analyst community in usage of modern data science tools
  • Drives common practice/process for data analysis and data discovery
  • Provides Lifetime greater visibility to the data science community
  • Expedites data visualization delivery by expanding current capabilities or introducing new tool sets
  • Matures the Life Time Data Lake environment to make data highly available for analysts and business partners
