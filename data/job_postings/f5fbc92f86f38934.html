Solution Strategist, Consultant (Clinical Optimization) - Oakland, CA

Reporting to the Senior Manager, Solution Strategist, Clinical Optimization, the Solution Strategist, Consultant is responsible for being Blue Shield of California’s front-line leader enabling clinical innovation on site with our key strategic partners. The Consultant will be part of a “tiger team” who partners directly with our state-wide provider partners define the future care delivery model, then implement new technologies, redesign process and change organizational structures to make the future a reality. The Consultant will also be responsible for partnering with our Provider Network Management team to baseline new provider partners’ capabilities and help develop improvement road maps tied to our mutual objectives. The Consultant will also participate cross-functionally in other larger enterprise projects as a subject matter expert to support broader health information technology strategies. Moreover, the Consultant may be responsible for leading a team of analysts, assigning and monitoring work of team members and providing guidance and leadership for other multidisciplinary efforts. This position may require statewide travel to Northern and Southern California locations.

Essential Responsibilities:
  • Provide inputs on business models, incentive systems and performance management tools. Support engagement with business owners to assess and evaluate specific recommendations around optimization
  • Defines highly complex business and customer needs and provides analysis toward operational efficiency and drives change within the organization
  • Uses system thinking to facilitate the transformation of new innovative capabilities into the care delivery model and fundamentally shift the cost, quality or experience from the model
  • Leads or participates in customer facing focus groups to document end to end customer journey and needs
  • Support product development across innovation lifecycle, based on findings in provider operations
  • Develop Use Cases and identify technical solutions to address opportunities to transform provider and payer operations
  • Support product development across innovation lifecycle, including:
  • Contributing innovative product ideas to the care model
  • Developing new product requirements and use cases and design documents
  • Develops business cases ROI analysis, feasibility, price modeling to provide new product recommendations to management
  • Lead a process of hypothesis testing and learning to determine ability to deliver new capabilities to the care delivery model
  • Hands on delivery support on major transformation projects and successful transitions to implementation support teams
  • Presentation skills with a high degree of comfort with both large and small audiences
  • Keeps a pulse on health market technology trends and emerging and evolving technologies
  • Maintains a keen understanding of broader provider and market trends
  • Identifies external parties to connect with to enhance Blue Shield’s product offerings
  • Drives strong collaboration with business stakeholders to define product strategy, business requirements, use cases, and new capabilities
  • Develops methodology for forecasting of benefits and guides prioritization of efforts based on impact, cost and ease of implementation

Qualifications

  • Requires a BA/BS in Business, Finance, Economics, Public Health, or Information Technology

  • Has advanced knowledge and skills including technical or functional expertise, business acumen, financial analysis skills, risk management, delegation skills, critical thinking and decision-making skills
  • Preferred experience in the design and implementation digital solutions and Connected Devices
  • Preferred technical experience in one or more of the following areas: Software design or development, Application Design, Systems Operations/Management, Database architecture, Virtualization
  • 5 years of Six Sigma or Lean experience preferred
  • Process optimization in a care delivery setting preferred
  • EMR workflow optimization preferred
  • Advanced level of Data Analysis, Dashboards
  • Knowledgeable with Alternative Payment Models and Value Based Purchasing
  • Proficiency in driving cross-functional projects
  • Knowledge of change management concepts and techniques
  • Superb communication skills, proficient oral and written presentation
  • Translate complex concepts into simple consumable artifacts
  • Proficiency in navigating complex programs and driving/collaborating on components to support the larger scope
