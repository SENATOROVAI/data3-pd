What you'll do:

  • Build, deploy and optimize machine learning models to improve traveler experience:
  • Build Cross-sell recommendation function for visitors based on user segmentation /destination /Amenities /seasonality, etc

  • Provide mentorship, peer review, and thought leadership across the business in the areas of statistical methods, experimentation, and tools

  • Intellectual curiosity and desire to learn new things, techniques and technologies

  • Excellent interpersonal skills, ability to present and write reports, strong partnership and collaboration skills
