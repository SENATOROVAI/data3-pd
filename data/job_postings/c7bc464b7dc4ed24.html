About the Role

The Williams-Sonoma Marketing Strategy & Analytics team is seeking a Senior Data Scientist to help develop, analyze, and execute our new Marketing Mix Modeling and Multi-Touch Attribution solution. This person will be involved in all aspects of digital, email, and direct mail marketing across a variety of brands, channels, and strategies. The focus of the role is to support revenue growth and improved marketing ROI with actionable insights and data analyses. This person will interact regularly with vendors, team members, cross-channel representatives, and the Williams-Sonoma leadership team as needed.

You're excited about this opportunity because you will...

  • Be a subject matter expert on all things related to Marketing Mix Modeling (MMM) and Multi-Touch Attribution (MTA)
  • Collaborate with marketers on the overall investment strategy with a keen focus on innovation, personalization, and acquisition
  • Conduct analyses on the effectiveness and efficiency of digital, email, and direct mail marketing programs
  • Lead ad-hoc analyses to standardize reporting and insights for cross-functional teams
  • Simplify and translate complex statistics to a wide range of audiences such as management, key stakeholders, peers, and non-technical groups
  • Maintain meticulous documentation on test designs, data methodologies, MMM, and MTA

About Williams-Sonoma, Inc.

Founded in 1956, Williams-Sonoma, Inc. is the premier specialty retailer of high-quality products for the kitchen and home in the United States. Our family of brands are Williams-Sonoma, Pottery Barn, Pottery Barn Kids, PBteen, West Elm, Williams-Sonoma Home, Rejuvenation, and Mark and Graham. These brands are among the best known and most respected in the industry. We offer beautifully-designed, stylish and functional products for every area of the home, including the kitchen, living room, bedroom, home office, closet, laundry room and even outdoor spaces. We've seen some big changes since our first brick-and-mortar store opened more than half of a century ago. What hasn't changed is our passion for high-quality products, functional design, outstanding customer service, and enhancing the lives of our customers and the communities where we operate. Today, we're a multi-brand, multi-channel, global enterprise supported by state-of-the-art technology and some of the most talented teams in retailing - and we're always looking for new energy and ideas.
