Data Science And Resource Manager - Golden, CO 80401

Wowza Media Systems is currently seeking a Data Science & Resource Manager to architect, administer and manage Wowza’s internal data structure and facilitate the company’s ongoing business data reporting needs.

This position will play a critical role in allowing Wowza’s leaders to make informed, data driven decisions. Take a front row seat in not only learning how Wowza is leading the video streaming industry but also influencing methods and best practices to enable forward progress and growth

The ideal candidate will demonstrate initiative, self-motivation and critical thinking skills and have substantial experience with complex data structures and technical environments

What you will be doing:

  • Work within the Data Operations & Systems Development teams to define and implement a robust and secure data warehouse environment
  • Formulating management techniques for quality data collection and implementing secure procedures for data management and analysis
  • Create repeatable, scalable processes for recurring and ad hoc data request with a focus on timeliness, efficiency and accuracy
  • Monitor and evaluate information and data systems to discover ways of enhancing (new technologies, tools, upgrades, etc.)
  • Partner closely with Business Intelligence & Corporate Strategy leads to enable data driven decision making at all levels in the company
  • Research and advise on optimal data structure and metrics reporting methodology

To be successful, you must have the ability to drive a project from start to completion, be very detail-oriented, be a self-starter, and have excellent communication skills. The ability to work well cross-functionally and with remote personnel is also required.

Requirements

  • Excellent understanding of data administration and management functions and familiarity with modern a complex data environment
  • Degree in IT, Computer Science, Data Science or related field
  • Proficiency in MS Office, Tableau, Salesforce, SQL/MySQL and Elasticsearch
  • Proven track record of self-motivation and initiative with ability to effectively manage multiple simultaneous projects
  • Strong interpersonal skills and ability to build rapport with people of all types
  • Work experience in Video Streaming Software or related industry a plus
  • Background in Finance and/or Database administration certifications a plus
